# SEBI LODR (Last Minute View)

## 📋 APPLICABILITY

### Listed Entities
- **Equity Shares** (Main Board/SME/IGP)
- **Non-Convertible Debt Securities**
- **Indian Depository Receipts (IDRs)**
- **Securitised Debt Instruments**
- **Security Receipts**
- **Mutual Fund Units**

### Exemptions (Reg 15)
- **Small Entities**: Paid-up ≤ ₹10 Cr + Net Worth ≤ ₹25 Cr
- **SME Exchange Listed**
- Once applicable → continues even if falls below threshold for **3 consecutive years**

## 👥 BOARD OF DIRECTORS (Reg 17)

### Composition

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Women Director** | ≥ 1 (Mandatory) |
| **Independent Women** | Top 1000: By Apr 1, 2020 |
| **Non-Executive** | ≥ 50% |
| **Independent Directors** | Non-Exec Chairman: ≥ 1/3rd<br>Exec Chairman/Promoter Chairman: ≥ 1/2 |
| **Minimum Size of directors** | Top 2000: ≥ 6 Directors (From Apr 1, 2020) |

### Key Limits
- **Age Limit**: Non-Exec > 75 years → Special Resolution required
- **Max Directorships**: ≤ 7 Listed Entities (IDs: ≤ 7, WTD: ≤ 3 as ID)
    - Listing here means equity listing only
- **Committee Membership**: ≤ 10 (Chairperson: ≤ 5)
  - *Only Audit + Stakeholder Committees count*
  - Across all the entities - Only Equity Listed

### Meetings
- **Frequency**: ≥ 4/year | Gap ≤ 120 days
    - Same as Co Act
- **Quorum (Top 2000)**: 1/3rd or 3 (whichever higher) + ≥ 1 ID

### Approval Requirements
- **Shareholder Approval**: Within 3 months of appointment
- **Special Resolution**: Annual remuneration > ₹5 Cr or 2.5% profits (Promoter/Exec Directors)

## 🔍 AUDIT COMMITTEE (Reg 18)

### Composition

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Members** | ≥ 3 Directors |
| **Independent** | ≥ 2/3rd (All IDs for SR Equity Shares) |
| **Financial Literacy** | 100% members |
| **Financial Expertise** | ≥ 1 member |
| **Chairperson** | ID (Present at AGM) |

### Meetings
- **Frequency**: ≥ 4/year | Gap ≤ 120 days
- **Quorum**: 2 or 1/3rd (whichever higher) + ≥ 2 IDs

### Key Roles
- 📊 Review Financial Results (Quarterly/Annual)
- 💼 Approve Related Party Transactions (Only IDs vote)
- 🔎 Monitor Auditor Independence
- 📈 Review Risk Management
- 🏦 Review Subsidiary Investments (> ₹100 Cr or 10% asset)

---

## 💰 NOMINATION & REMUNERATION COMMITTEE (Reg 19)

### Composition

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Members** | ≥ 3 (All Non-Executive) |
| **Independent** | ≥ 2/3rd |
| **Chairperson** | ID (Company Chair can be member, NOT Chair) |

### Meetings
- **Frequency**: ≥ 1/year
- **Quorum**: 2 or 1/3rd (whichever higher) + ≥ 1 ID

### Key Functions
- 👤 Identify & Recommend Directors/KMPs
- 📋 Formulate Evaluation Criteria for IDs
- 💵 Recommend Remuneration Policy
- 🎯 Evaluate ID Performance (Board removes evaluated IDs)

## 🤝 STAKEHOLDERS RELATIONSHIP COMMITTEE (Reg 20)

### Composition
- **Members**: ≥ 3 (≥ 1 ID)
- **Chairperson**: Non-Executive Director
- **Meetings**: ≥ 1/year
- **For SR Equity**: ≥ 2/3rd IDs

### Scope
- 📧 Resolve Investor Grievances
- 🗳️ Review Voting Rights Exercise
- 📜 Review RTA Service Standards
- 💸 Monitor Unclaimed Dividends

## ⚠️ RISK MANAGEMENT COMMITTEE (Reg 21)

### Applicability
- **Top 1000 Listed Entities** (By Market Cap)
- **High Value Debt Listed Entities** (Outstanding debt > ₹500 Cr)

### Composition

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Members** | ≥ 3 (Majority: Board Members) |
| **Independent** | ≥ 1 (SR Equity: ≥ 2/3rd IDs) |
| **Chairperson** | Board Member |

### Meetings
- **Frequency**: ≥ 2/year | Gap ≤ 180 days
- **Quorum**: 2 or 1/3rd (whichever higher) + ≥ 1 Board Member

### Key Roles
- 🛡️ Formulate Risk Management Policy (incl. Cyber Security)
- 📊 Review Risk Mitigation Systems
- 🔄 Policy Review: ≥ Once every 3 years

## 🔗 RELATED PARTY TRANSACTIONS (RPT) (Reg 23)

### Definition of Related Party
- **Companies Act 2013**: Section 2(76)
- **Additional (LODR)**: 
  - Promoter/Promoter Group
  - Holding ≥ 20% (from Apr 1, 2023: ≥ 10%)

### Materiality Thresholds

| Transaction Type | Threshold |
|------------------|-----------|
| **General RPT** | ₹1000 Cr OR 10% Annual Consolidated Turnover (Lower) |
| **Brand/Royalty** | 5% Annual Consolidated Turnover |

### Approval Requirements

#### Audit Committee
- **Who Approves**: Only IDs
- **Omnibus Approval**: Valid 1 year (Repetitive transactions, ≤ ₹1 Cr if unforeseen)
- **Review**: Quarterly

#### Shareholders
- **Material RPTs**: Prior approval via Ordinary Resolution
- **Related Parties**: Cannot vote
- **Exception**: Insolvency Resolution Plan (Section 31)

### Exemptions
- Govt Company ↔ Govt Company
- Holding ↔ Wholly Owned Subsidiary (Consolidated accounts)
- Between 2 Wholly Owned Subsidiaries

### Disclosure
- **Frequency**: Every 6 months (within 15 days of financial results)
- **From Apr 1, 2023**: On date of publication

---

## 🏢 MATERIAL SUBSIDIARY (Reg 24)

### Definition
- Income/Net Worth > **20%** of Consolidated Income/Net Worth

### Requirements
- **≥ 1 ID** on Board of Unlisted Material Subsidiary
- **Audit Committee**: Review subsidiary's financials & investments
- **Board**: Place subsidiary's Board minutes
- **Significant Transactions**: Report (> 10% revenue/expenses/assets/liabilities)

### Shareholder Approval (Special Resolution)
- **Disposal**: Shareholding ≤ 50% or loss of control
- **Sale/Lease**: > 20% assets in FY
- **Exception**: Court/NCLT approved schemes (Disclose within 1 day)

## 🔔 DISCLOSURE OF MATERIAL EVENTS (Reg 30)

### Materiality Guidelines
1. Omission → Discontinuity/Alteration in public info
2. Omission → Significant market reaction if disclosed later
3. Board's opinion (if above not applicable)

### Timeline
- **General**: Within 24 hours
- **Board Outcomes**: Within 30 minutes of meeting closure
- **Delay**: Explanation mandatory

### Para A (Deemed Material - No Guidelines)
- 🔀 Acquisitions/Mergers/Schemes
- 💹 Securities Issuance/Buyback/Forfeiture
- ⭐ Rating Revisions
- 🎯 Board Outcomes (Dividends/Buyback/Fund Raise/Results)
- 📜 Agreements (Shareholder/JV/Family Settlement/Media)
- 🚨 Fraud/Arrest of KMP/Promoter/Default
- 👔 Change in Directors/KMP/Auditor/Compliance Officer
- 📝 Auditor/ID Resignation (Details + Letter within 24 hrs / 7 days)
- 🏦 Resolution Plan/Restructuring/OTS/BIFR/Winding-up
- 📊 Notices/Circulars to Shareholders
- 🗳️ AGM/EGM Proceedings
- 📑 MoA/AoA Amendments
- 📈 Analyst Meets Schedule + Post-Earnings Call Recordings (Audio/Video: 24 hrs, Transcript: 5 days)
- ⚖️ CIRP Events (16 sub-events)
- 🔬 Forensic Audit Initiation + Final Report

### Para B (Apply Materiality Guidelines)
- 🏭 Production Commencement/Postponement
- 🔄 Business Nature Change
- 📦 Capacity Addition/Product Launch
- 📋 Major Contracts/Agreements
- 🌪️ Operational Disruption (Calamity/Strikes)
- ⚖️ Regulatory Changes Impact
- 🏛️ Litigation/Disputes
- 🎭 Fraud by Directors (non-KMP)/Employees
- 📊 ESOP/ESPS Options
- 🛡️ Guarantees/Indemnities
- 🔑 Key License Grant/Withdrawal

## 💵 FINANCIAL RESULTS (Reg 33)

### Timelines

| Period | Submission Timeline |
|--------|---------------------|
| **Quarterly (Q1-Q3)** | 45 days |
| **Q4/Annual** | 60 days |

### Requirements
- **Approval**: Board of Directors
- **Authentication**: Signed by Chair/MD/WTD/Authorized Director
- **Limited Review**: For unaudited results
- **Audit Report**: For annual results (+ Statement on Audit Qualifications if modified opinion)

### Modified Opinion Disclosure
- **Impact on**: Profit/Loss, Net Worth, Total Assets, Turnover, EPS, Liabilities
- **Management's View**: Explain + Actions taken

### Consolidated Results
- **Mandatory**: If subsidiary exists
- **Coverage**: ≥ 80% of Revenue/Assets/Profits audited/reviewed

### Publication
- **Newspaper**: Within 48 hours of Board approval
- **Format**: Consolidated + Standalone footnote (Turnover/PBT/PAT)

---

## 📰 ANNUAL REPORT (Reg 34)

### Submission Timeline
- **To Exchange + Website**: Before dispatch to shareholders
- **To Shareholders**: ≥ 21 days before AGM
- **Revised Report**: Within 48 hours of AGM (if changes)

### Contents

#### Financial Statements
- ✅ Audited Standalone + Consolidated
- ✅ Cash Flow Statement (Indirect Method)
- ✅ Audit Report + Statement on Audit Qualifications
- ✅ Directors' Report

#### Corporate Governance Report (Schedule V)
- 🧭 Philosophy on Governance
- 👥 Board Composition (Category/Attendance/Relationships)
- 🔍 Committee Details (Audit/NRC/SRC/RMC)
- 💰 Director Remuneration
- 🗳️ General Body Meetings (Last 3 AGMs)
- 📧 Communication Means
- 📊 Shareholding Info
- ⚠️ Non-Compliances/Penalties
- 🔔 Vigil Mechanism Confirmation
- ⭐ Credit Ratings
- 🎓 ID Familiarization Programs
- 👤 Sexual Harassment Disclosures

#### Additional Disclosures
- 📈 Management Discussion & Analysis (MD&A)
- 💼 Business Responsibility Report (Top 1000)
  - From FY 2022-23: **Business Responsibility & Sustainability Report (BRSR)**
- 📜 CEO/CFO Compliance Certificate
- 🎖️ Auditor/CS Certificate on Corporate Governance

## 🏦 DEBT SECURITIES (Chapter V)

### Applicability
- **High Value Debt**: Outstanding ≥ ₹500 Cr
- **Corporate Governance**: Comply or Explain (Till Mar 31, 2023) → Mandatory thereafter


### Financial Results
- **Quarterly**: Within 45 days (Last quarter: 60 days)
- **Audited Annual**: Within 60 days
- **Disclosures**: Debt-Equity, DSCR, ISCR, Current Ratio, etc.

### Security Cover (Reg 54)
- **Requirement**: 100% security cover OR higher (as per Debenture Trust Deed)
- **Disclosure**: Quarterly with financial results
- **Certificate**: Half-yearly by Statutory Auditor

### Credit Rating
- **Review**: ≥ Once/year by SEBI-registered CRA

### Debenture Trustees
- **Annual Report**: Forward promptly
- **Intimations**: Rating revisions, defaults, covenant breaches, charge creation
- **Certificate**: Half-yearly on security cover maintenance

### Payment Status Certificate
- **Timeline**: Within 1 working day of due date
- **Quarterly Details**: 5 working days before quarter + 7 days after quarter

## 🔄 SCHEMES OF ARRANGEMENT (Reg 37)

### Process
1. **Draft Scheme**: File with Exchange (with fee)
2. **Stock Exchange Review**: Within 30 days
3. **No-Objection Letter**: Valid 6 months
4. **NCLT Filing**: Within validity period
5. **Post-Sanction**: Submit documents within prescribed timeline

### Fee (Schedule XI)
- **Rate**: 0.1% of higher paid-up capital (Listed/Transferee/Resulting)
- **Cap**: ₹5 Lakh

### Exceptions (No Approval Needed)
- **Wholly Owned Subsidiary → Holding** (Only disclosure)
- **Insolvency Resolution Plan** (Section 31) (Disclose within 1 day)

3. ✅ **Meeting Frequencies & Quorums**
4. ✅ **RPT Approval Process** (Audit Committee vs Shareholders)

9. ✅ **Max Directorships/Committees**

### Common Traps
- ⚠️ **Working Days vs Calendar Days**
- ⚠️ **Excluding Date of Intimation & Event**
- ⚠️ **Top 500 vs Top 1000 vs Top 2000** (Different requirements)
- ⚠️ **Once Applicable Continues for 3 Years** (Even if falls below threshold)
- ⚠️ **IDs Only Vote for RPT Approval** (Not full Audit Committee)

## 🔑 MNEMONIC DEVICES

### Board Composition: **"WINE"**
- **W**omen Director (≥ 1)
- **I**ndependent Directors (1/3rd or 1/2)
- **N**on-Executive (≥ 50%)
- **E**xecutive + Non-Executive (Optimum Mix)

### Audit Committee: **"3-2-1 FFL"**
- **3** Members
- **2/3rd** IDs
- **1** Financial Expert
- **F**inancially **F**iterate + **L**imited Review

### Timelines: **"24-30-45-60-21"**
- **24** hrs: Material Events
- **30** mins: Board Outcomes
- **45** days: Q1-Q3 Results
- **60** days: Annual Results
- **21** days: Quarterly Compliances

### RPT Thresholds: **"1-10-5-20"**
- **₹1000 Cr** OR **10%** Turnover (Material RPT)
- **5%** Turnover (Brand/Royalty)
- **20%** Income/Net Worth (Material Subsidiary)
