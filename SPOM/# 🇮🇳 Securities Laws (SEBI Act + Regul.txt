# 🇮🇳 Securities Laws (SEBI Act + Regulations)

## 🏛️ SEBI Act, 1992

### Board Composition (Sec 4) 🎓
- **Formula:** 1 + 2 + 1 + 5 = 9 Members
- **Chairman** (Appointed by CG)
- **2 Members** (Ministry of Finance officials)
- **1 Member** (RBI official)
- **5 Members** (Appointed by CG)
  - *Must include at least 3 Whole Time Members (WTM)*
- **Tenure:** Max 5 years, eligible for re-appointment. Max age 65.
- **Termination:** 3 months notice or salary in lieu (by CG or Member).

### Powers & Functions (Sec 11)
- **3 Core Duties:**
  1. Protect Interests (Investors)
  2. Promote Development (Market)
  3. Regulate Business (Intermediaries)
- **Civil Court Powers:** Discovery of books, Summoning, Inspection.
- **Enforcement:**
  - Suspend trading 🚫
  - Impound proceeds 💰
  - Attach accounts (Max 90 days, needs Spl Court confirmation) ⏳
  - Cecase & Desist orders (Sec 11D)

### ⚠️ Penalties (Sec 15A - 15HB)
| Section | Default | Penalty Amt (🎓 Shortcut) |
|:---:|---|---|
| **15A** | Return/Info/Books | 1L/Day $\rightarrow$ Max 1 Cr |
| **15B** | Broker-Client Agmt | 1L/Day $\rightarrow$ Max 1 Cr |
| **15C** | Investor Grievance | 1L/Day $\rightarrow$ Max 1 Cr |
| **15G** | **Insider Trading** | Min 10L $\rightarrow$ Max 25 Cr **OR** 3x Profit |
| **15H** | **Takeover Discl.** | Min 10L $\rightarrow$ Max 25 Cr **OR** 3x Profit |
| **15HA**| Fraud/Unfair Trade | Min 5L $\rightarrow$ Max 25 Cr **OR** 3x Profit |
| **15HB**| Residual (Others) | Min 1L $\rightarrow$ Max 1 Cr |

### ⚖️ SAT (Securities Appellate Tribunal)
- **Composition:** Presiding Officer + Judicial Member + Tech Member.
- **Appeal Time:** Within **45 Days** of SEBI Order ⏳.
- **Further Appeal:** To Supreme Court (Only on Law Qs) within **60 Days** ⏳.

## 📋 SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015

### Common Obligations
- **Key Personnel:** Qualified CS as Compliance Officer 🎓.
- **Share Transfer:** Appoint Agent (RTA) or In-house facility.
- **Grievance:** File report with Stock Exch (SE) within **21 days** of quarter end.

### 🏢 Board & Committees (Comparison)
| Feature | Audit Comm (Reg 18) | NRC (Reg 19) | SRC (Reg 20) | RMC (Reg 21) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Min Members** | 3 Directors | 3 Directors | 3 Directors | 3 Members (Maj. BOD) |
| **Composition** | 2/3rd Indep. | All NED, 2/3rd Indep. | At least 1 Indep. | Maj. BOD + 1 Indep. |
| **Chairperson** | Indep. Director | Indep. Director | NED | BOD Member |
| **Meetings** | 4/Year (Gap 120d) | 1/Year | 1/Year | 2/Year |
| **Quorum** | 2 or 1/3rd (Min 2 Indep) | 2 or 1/3rd (Min 1 Indep) | - | 2 or 1/3rd (Min 1 BOD) |

### Other Corporate Governance
- **Independent Directors:** Min 1/3rd (if Chair is Non-Exec) OR 1/2 (if Chair is Exec/Promoter).
- **Woman Director:** At least 1 (Top 500/1000 need Indep Woman Dir).
- **Subsidiary (Reg 24):** Material if Income/Net Worth > 10% of Consol.
  - *Restriction:* Cannot sell >50% shares without Spl Resolution.

## 🚀 SEBI (ICDR) Regulations, 2018

### 📊 IPO Eligibility (Reg 6) - "3-15-1-50 Rule" 🎓
1. **Net Tangible Assets:** $\ge$ 3 Cr (Max 50% monetary).
2. **Op. Profit:** $\ge$ 15 Cr (Avg last 3 yrs).
3. **Net Worth:** $\ge$ 1 Cr (Last 3 yrs).
4. **Name Change:** If yes, 50% revenue from new activity.
- *If failing above:* Book Building Mandatory + 75% to QIBs.

### Promoters' Contribution (Reg 14)
- **Minimum:** 20% of Post-Issue Capital.
- **Lock-in:**
  - 20% Contribution: **18 Months** ⏳ (3 Years if Capex majority).
  - Excess over 20%: **6 Months** ⏳ (1 Year if Capex majority).

### Pricing & Allocation
- **Book Building:**
  - **QIB:** Min 75% (if Alt route) / Max 50% (Normal).
  - **Retail:** Min 35% (Normal) / Max 10% (Alt route).
  - **NII (HNI):** 15%.
- **Price Band:** Cap price $\le$ 120% of Floor Price.

## 🤝 SEBI (SAST / Takeover) Regs, 2011

### 🚦 Trigger Points (Open Offer)
- **Initial Trigger:** Acquirer + PAC acquiring shares/voting rights $\ge$ **25%**.
- **Creeping Acquisition:** Already holding $\ge$ 25% but $<$ 75%.
  - *Trigger:* Acquiring **> 5%** in a Financial Year.
- **Control:** Acquisition of "Control" irrespective of shareholding.

### Open Offer Details
- **Offer Size:** At least **26%** of total shares.
- **Escrow Acct:**
  - First 500 Cr $\rightarrow$ 25% Cash margin.
  - Balance $\rightarrow$ 10% Cash margin.

## 🕵️‍♂️ SEBI (PIT / Insider Trading) Regs, 2015

### 🔑 Key Definitions
- **UPSI:** Unpublished Price Sensitive Info (Financials, Dividends, Mergers).
- **Connected Person:** Associated 6 months prior, Directors, Employees.
- **Insider:** Connected Person OR Someone involved having UPSI access.

### 🚫 Restrictions
- **Communication:** Don't share UPSI (Except: Legitimate Purpose like Audit/Due Diligence).
- **Trading:** Don't trade while in possession of UPSI.

### Disclosures (Reg 7)
- **Initial:** Promoter/KMP/Director $\rightarrow$ Disclose holding within 7 days of appt.
- **Continual:** If transaction > **₹10 Lakhs** in a quarter.
  - Insider $\rightarrow$ Co: 2 Trading Days.
  - Co $\rightarrow$ Stock Exch: 2 Trading Days.
