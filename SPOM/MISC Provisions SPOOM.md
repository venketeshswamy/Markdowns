## 🔍 1. Registered Valuers (Sec 247)
### 📋 Scope & Assets
- **Assets Valued:** Property, stocks, shares, debentures, securities, goodwill, net worth. - Any other assets
- **Appointment:** By Audit Committee or Board of Directors (BOD only in absence of AC).

### 👮 Role & Conduct
- **Key Duties:** Impartiality, due diligence, true & fair valuation.
- **Conflict of Interest:** 🚫 Cannot value assets if direct/indirect interest exists (3 years prior or post-appointment).
### Eligibility and Qualifications

#### 🎓 Qualifications (Rule 4)
- **Education + Experience:**
    - PG Degree/Diploma + 3 Years exp.
    - Bachelor’s Degree + 5 Years exp.
    - Professional Member (e.g., CA/CS) + 3 Years exp.
- **Requirements:** 
    - Resident in India, 
    - Sound mind, not Bankrupt
    - Passed Valuation Exam, Member of Valuation association
- Disqualifications
    - 6 Months Jail - 5 year cool off || More than 7 years jail, Permanent Ban 
    - 271J of Income Tax Act- 5 years ban (Misrepresentation by CA/Valuers)
- Partnership or Companies - eligibility
    - 3 or all (To be Registered Valuers) - Partners
    - No partner to be disqualified
### Registration Process
- Application in Form A or B (Individual and Partnership)
    - Fees 5K and 10K
- Authority checks eligibility
    - Additional docs can be asked in 21 Days
    - To issue within 60 days of application
    - If reject,
        - 15 days for applicant to give any other explanations
        - So 45 days la give reason for rejection
### **Conduct of Valuation**
- As per notified valuation standards 
- if obtaining inputs from other pros-> You can, but liability is yours only- 
- State in report
    - Background, Purpose of valuation, identity of valuer
    - Experts used
    - Independence
    - Other important matters if any 
    - Conclusion and caveats, limitations etc of valuation

## 🚫 2. Removal of Names (Strike Off) (Sec 248-252)
### 🏛️ By Registrar (RoC) - Sec 248(1)
- **Grounds:**
    - Failed to commence business within **1 year** of incorporation.
    - Not carrying on business for **2 immediately preceding FYs** (and not applied for Dormant Status).
    - Subscribers to Memo  $\to$ Not paid the Subscription money $\to$ Declaration to such effect is not given
    - After Physical Verification, revealed that company is not doing any business or operations
- **Process:** Notice to Company/Directors $\to$ 30 days for representation.

### 🏢 Voluntary by Company - Sec 248(2)
- **Prerequisites:** Extinguish all liabilities.
- **Approval:** Special Resolution OR Consent of 75% members (paid-up capital).
- **Regulators:** Approval required if regulated by Special Act.
- Section 8 companies cannot apply for voluntary strike off

### ⚠️ Restrictions on Voluntary Strike off (Sec 249)
- **Cannot apply if in previous 3 months:**
    - Changed name or shifted registered office (state-to-state).
    - Disposed of property for gain (not normal course).
    - Engaged in activity other than filing application.
    - Pending Tribunal compromise/arrangement.

### 🛑 Effect of Dissolution (Sec 250)
- Ceases to operate as a company.
- Certificate of Incorporation cancelled.
- **Note:** Liability of directors/managers continues.
### Removal of Name- Fraudulent?  
- Intention of filing application was to 
    - Defraud Creditors? or evade liabilities?
        - Mgmt is joint and severally liable
        - Registrar may recommend for prosecution 

### ⚖️ Appeal to Tribunal (Sec 252)
- **Who can apply?**
  - Person aggrieved
  - Registrar of Companies (RoC)
  - Company / Member / Creditor / Workman
- **Opportunity of Being Heard (OOBH): for removal of name not being justified**
  - RoC
  - Company
  - All concerned persons
- **Time Limits:**
  - Within **3 years** from RoC’s order (Person aggrieved / RoC)
  - Within **20 years** from Gazette notice (Member / Creditor / Workman)
- Order of tribunal to be filed within 30 days to ROC


## 🏛️ 3. Government Companies (Sec 394-395)
### 📝 Definition
- $\ge$ **51%** Paid-up Share Capital held by:
    - Central Government.
    - State Government(s).
    - Partly Central + Partly State.
- *Includes Subsidiary of a Gov Company.*

### 📊 Annual Reports
- **Timeline:** Prepared within **3 months** of AGM.
- **Process:**
    1.  Comments by CAG (Comptroller & Auditor General).
    2.  Audit Report.
    3.  Laid before **Parliament** (if Central) or **State Legislature** (if State).
- Even applies to Govt company in liquidation

## 💰 4. Nidhi Companies (Sec 406 + Rules)
### 💡 Core Concept
- **Definition:** "Mutual Benefit Society".
- **Object:** Cultivate thrift & savings amongst members.
- **Rule:** Lending/Borrowing restricted to **members only**.
### Parliamentary Requirement
    - Every notification proposed towards any change in application of sections shall be 
        - Passed in each Parliament house
        - Laid in both houses for 30 days
            - Exclude the days if adjournment is more than 4 consecutive days
### 🏗️ Incorporation Requirements
- **Type:** Public Company.
- **Name:** Must end with "Nidhi Limited".
- **Min Equity Capital:** ₹10 Lakhs.
- **Prohibited:** Issuing Preference Shares.
- Deposit and Lending to members only
    - Except: Up to 20% of Gross Nidhi Income, Locker Rent income can be there


### 📉 Limits & Ratios (Within 1 Year)
- **Members:** Min 200.
- **Net Owned Funds (NOF):** Min ₹20 Lakhs.
- **Ratio (NOF : Deposits):** Max 1:20.
- **Unencumbered Term Deposits:** Min 10% of outstanding deposits.

### Filing of Forms
- NDH -1 - Return of Stat Compliances
    - To be Filed within 90 days of close of Financial year
    - Certified by CA/CS/CMA in practice
- NDH - 2 - 
    - Asking for extension of time for the limits above     
    - RD approves it 
        - Extension up to 1 year of application
            - Even after 2 years, (Viz, extension) - Cannot comply?
                - Stopped from Accepting deposits
                - Financial Penalties liable
    - To be filed within 30 days of Completion of FY


### 🚫 Prohibitions (Rule 6)
- Chit Fund, Hire Purchase, Insurance.
- Opening Current Accounts.
- Acquiring another company (control).
- Advertising for deposits.
- Only 20% of GTI as locker rent allowed

### Share Capital - Nidhis
- Should be of FV at least 10 each 
    - Except for the Old companies act nidhis
- Should be fully paid up shares
- Minimum 10 shares to be alloted (or FV 100 Rs)
    - Except : If RD or Savings account is held, then 1 share
- Cannot ask for service fees
### Membership rules
- Body Corporate or Trust **Cannot** be Member
- Minor cannot be member
- 200 members should be there
- If you have deposit or loan, you CANNOT transfer more than 50% of the shares
    - Also to be ensured that if 50% of shares are transferred, the 10 shares or 100 FV limit should be respected

## 💤 5. Dormant Company (Sec 455)
### 🎯 Eligibility
- Formed for **Future Project** OR to hold **Asset/Intellectual Property**.
- **No Significant Accounting Transaction (SAT).**
- Suo moto by ROC if 2 years FS not filed. 

### 📉 Inactive Company Status
- Not carrying business/operations.
- OR No SAT in last **2 FYs**.
- OR Not filed Financial Statements/Annual Returns for **2 FYs**.

### 📝 Significant Accounting Transaction (SAT)
- **Includes:** Any transaction **EXCEPT**:
    1. Payment of fees to RoC.
    2. Statutory payments.
    3. Allotment of shares.
    4. Office/Records maintenance payments.

### 🔄 Compliance
- **Min Directors:** Public (3), Private (2), OPC (1).
- **Return:** File **MSC-3** annually within 30 days of FY end.
- **Expiry:** If dormant for **5 consecutive years** $\to$ Strike Off.
- if any act done which affects dormancy
    - Within 7 days of such act, file for Active Status

### Other rules for getting dormant company status
- No inspection, inquiry or investigation is called forward
- No prosecution against the company
- No loan outstanding (If outstanding, lender approval)
- No dispute regarding management
- No Default in workman dues
- Securities are not listed
### Process
- Pass SR
- File with ROCs
- Certificate provided
- Annual return of dormant company to be filed within 30 days of end of FY
### Form Tip

## 🚨 6. Fraud & General Penalties
### ☠️ Fraud Punishment (Sec 447)
| Scenario | Amount Criteria | Imprisonment | Fine | Clause |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Major Fraud** | $\ge$ ₹10L OR 1% Turnover | 6 months - 10 years | 1x - 3x amount | AND|
| **Public Interest** | Involved | **Min 3 years** - 10 years | 1x - 3x amount | AND |
| **Minor Fraud** | < ₹10L AND < 1% Turnover | Up to 5 years | Up to ₹50 Lakhs | OR |

### ⚖️ General Penalty (Sec 450)
- *Applied when no specific penalty exists.*
- **Company/Officer:** ₹10,000 + ₹1,000 per day (continuing default).
- **Max Cap:** ₹2 Lakhs (Company) / ₹50,000 (Officer).
