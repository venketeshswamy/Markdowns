# Prevention of Oppression & Mismanagement (Ch XVI)

## 📌 Introduction
### Concept
*   **Oppression**: Burdensome, harsh, wrongful conduct; lack of probity
    - It is not single isolated act, it is continuous act.
*   **Mismanagement**: Material change prejudicial to Co/Public interest
*   **Rule of Majority** (Foss v. Harbottle)
    *   Majority rules; Courts usually don't interfere
    *   **Exceptions**: Ultra-vires, Fraud on minority, Special majority req.

## Sec 241: Application to Tribunal
### ✅ Compliance
*   **Who can apply?**
    *   Any Member (Meeting Sec 244 criteria) complaining
    *   Central Government (Suo moto)
        - Grounds
            - Fraud, Negligence, default etc
            - Business with unsound principles
            - Prejudicial to industry 
            - Intend to defraud
        - Application to contain
            - Concise statement of particulars in writing
            - Signed and should be verified as per CRPC
*   **Grounds for Complaint**
    *   Affairs conducted in manner:
        *   Prejudicial to Public Interest
        *   Prejudicial/Oppressive to Member(s)
        *   Prejudicial to Company's Interest
    *   **Material Change** in Management/Control
        *   Alteration in BOD/Manager/Ownership
        *   Likely to prejudice Co/Members
    *   **Past Acts** covered

## Sec 244: Right to Apply (Eligibility)
### 📉 Scope (Thresholds)
*   **Co with Share Capital**
     -   Fulfill any of condition
            - Member Count basis
                *   📉 **Condition 1**: Min 100 Members
                *   📉 **Condition 2**: Min 1/10th of Total Members
            - Share capital basis
                *   📉 **Condition 3**: Holding Min 1/10th Issued Share Capital
                    *   ⚠️ Must have paid all calls/sums due
                    - Calculation is based on
                        - ISSUED CAPITAL
                        - NOT PAID UP CAPITAL
*   **Co without Share Capital**
    *   📉 Min 1/5th of Total Members
*   **Waiver of this threshold by tribunal**
    *   Tribunal may waive requirements on application
*   **Joint Holders**
    *   Counted as 1 Member

## Sec 242: Powers of Tribunal
### ✅ Compliance
*   **Conditions for Order**
    *   Affairs are prejudicial/oppressive
    *   Winding up Paradox (Company in situation of winding up, but winding up causes harm to shareholders)
*   **Nature of Orders**
    *   Regulate future conduct of affairs
    *   Purchase of shares (by Co/Members) -> Reduction of Capital
    *   Restrictions on transfer/allotment
    *   Terminate/Modify agreements (MD/Manager/Director)
    *   Set aside transfers/delivery of goods (made within 3 months)
    *   Remove MD/Manager/Director
    *   Recovery of undue gains
*   **Post-Order Process**
    *   📝 File Certified Copy with ROC within ⏳ 30 days
        - File the order
        - File the altered memorandum (If applicable based on tribunal order) within 30 days
    *   Interim Order allowed
    *   Alteration of MOA/AOA requires Tribunal Leave
        - AOA/MOA if altered by tribunal, it cannot be changed by members

### 🚔 Liability (Contravention of Order)
*   **Company**
    *   💰 Min ₹1 Lakh - Max ₹25 Lakhs
*   **Officer in Default**
    *   💰 Min ₹25,000 - Max ₹1 Lakh
    *   *(Imprisonment provision omitted)*

## Sec 243: Consequences of Termination
### ✅ Compliance
*   **Effect of Order (Sec 242)**
    *   Terminates/Modifies agreement
    *   **No Claim** for damages/compensation for loss of office
*   **Disqualification**
    *   Removed MD/Director/Manager
    *   Cannot hold office for ⏳ 5 Years
    *   ⚠️ Exception: With Tribunal Leave + CG Notice (Central govt notice is also mandatory)
*   **Fit & Proper Person (Sec 242(4A))**
    *   If decided not fit & proper: Disqualified for ⏳ 5 Years
    *   No compensation for loss of office

### 🚔 Liability
*   **Contravention (Acting as MD/Director)**
    *   Officer: 💰 Fine up to ₹5 Lakhs

## Sec 245: Class Action
### ✅ Compliance
*   **Applicants**: Members or Depositors
*   **Respondents**: Company, Directors, Auditors, Experts
*   **Grounds**: Affairs prejudicial to Co/Members/Depositors
*   **Reliefs Sought**
    *   Restrain Ultra Vires acts
    *   Restrain breach of MOA/AOA
    *   Declare resolution void (Suppression of facts)
    *   Claim damages/compensation
    *   Any remedy that tribunal deems fit
*   **Audit Firm Liability**
    *   Firm + Partners jointly & severally liable
    *   If misleading statement/fraudulent act

### 📉 Scope (Requisite Number)
*   **Members (Share Capital) (Should have paid all the calls)**
    *   📉 Min 100 Members OR 
    *    Min 5% of Total Members *(Lower of the two)*
    *   **OR**
    *   📉 Holding Min 5% Issued Capital (Unlisted)
    *   📉 Holding Min 2% Issued Capital (Listed)
*   **Members (No Share Capital)**
    *   📉 Min 1/5th of Total Members
*   **Depositors**
    *   📉 Min 100 Depositors OR Min 5% of Total Depositors *(Lower of the two)*
    *   **OR**
    *   📉 Owning Min 5% of Total Deposits
*   ⚠️ **Exemption from this section to**: Banking Companies
### Considerations 
- Application made in good faith?
- Any oppressive acts by directors as mentioned above for which relief is sought
- Unique remedy or strictly class action suit required?
- Shareholder views
### Way of class action suit
- Public notice to be served to all members and depositors of the class
    - Paid by the applicant first and then later recovered from oppressor
- All similar applications in any jurisdiction to be consolidated
    - Once a Class Action is admitted and the public notice is issued, no other application on the same subject matter can be filed in any court or tribunal.
- Tribunal has power to decide the lead applicant
- 2 class action suit for same action is not allowed - they will be consolidated
- Cost of expense of suit = to the oppressor
- Order of tribunal is binding to all persons 
### Cost of action suit
- Borne by oppressor
### 🚔 Liability
*   **Non-Compliance of Order**
    *   Co: 💰 Min ₹5 Lakhs - Max ₹25 Lakhs
    *   Officer: 💰 Min ₹25,000 - Max ₹1 Lakh AND/OR Imprisonment up to 3 Years
*   **Frivolous Application**
    *   💰 Max ₹1 Lakh (to opposite party)

### ✅ Compliance
*   **Provisions Applied Mutatis Mutandis**

    *   Sec 338: Liability where proper accounts not kept
    *   Sec 339: Liability for fraudulent conduct
    *   Sec 340: Damages against delinquent directors
    *   Sec 341: Liability extends to Partners/Directors
