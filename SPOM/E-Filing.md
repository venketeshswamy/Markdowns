# Chapter 10: e-Filing 

## MCA21 Overview
- **Mission**: e-Governance Project of MCA
- **Key Features**
  - Online Portal (Anytime/Anywhere)
  - **SRN**: Service Request Number (Tracking)
  - **DIN**: Director Identification Number
  - Public Access to Documents

## Statutory Provisions (S. 398-402)
- **S. 398**: Filing in Electronic Form
  - **Rule 7**: Format (PDF) & Stamp Duty (Scanned if physical)
- **Rule 8**: Authentication
  - **DSC**: Digital Signature Certificate (Class II/III)
  - **Liability**: Signatory & Professional responsible for correctness
  - **Deactivation**: Govt can deactivate DSC for false info
- **S. 400**: Electronic form can be **exclusive** or alternative

## e-Filing Process
- **Registration**: Create User ID on Portal
- **DSC Registration**: Register Director/Professional DSC
- **Form Completion**: Download -> Use **"Pre-fill"**
- **Pre-Scrutiny**: System checks completeness
- **Submission**: Upload -> Pay Fee -> SRN
  - *Payment Modes*: Card, Net Banking, Challan, Pay Later

## XBRL (Extensible Business Reporting Language)
- **Mandatory Filers**
  - Listed Companies
  - Paid-up Capital ≥ ₹5 Cr
  - Turnover ≥ ₹100 Cr
  - Ind AS Companies
- **Exemptions**
  - Banking, Insurance, NBFCs, Housing Finance
- **Continuity Rule**: Once XBRL, always XBRL

## Fees & Defaults (S. 403)
- **Late Filing**
  - **Additional Fee**: Min ₹100/day (Annual Returns)
  - **Higher Additional Fee**: For repeated defaults
- **Consequences**: Penalties for Company & Officers

## MCA21 Version 3.0
- **e-Scrutiny**: Central Cell flags forms for deep check
- **e-Adjudication**: Online hearings & digitized process
- **CMS**: Compliance Management System (Auto e-notices)
- **MCA Lab**: Analytics & AI for system improvement

## Case Study Learnings
- **Audit Duty**: Verify **Arm's Length** in Related Party Txns
- **Fraud**: Report diversion of funds to Central Govt
- **Deemed Public Co**: Pvt Subsidiary of Public Co must use **Demat**
- **Officer in Default**
  - Exec Directors liable
  - Non-Execs excused if not involved
