# 📜 SEBI ICDR Regulations, 2018 (Last Minute View)

## 🚦 IPO Eligibility (Reg 6)
### ✅ Profitability Route (Reg 6(1))
*   **Net Tangible Assets**
    *   Min **₹3 Cr** (in each of preceding 3 years)
    *   Max **50%** in Monetary Assets
    *   *Exception:* Limit not applicable if offer is **100% OFS**
*   **Operating Profit**
    *   Avg **₹15 Cr** (preceding 3 years)
    *   Must be profit in **each** of 3 years
*   **Net Worth**
    *   Min **₹1 Cr** (each of preceding 3 years)
*   **Name Change?**
    *   If changed in last 1 yr -> **50% revenue** from new activity

### 🛑 QIB Route (Reg 6(2))
*   *If 6(1) conditions NOT met:*
    *   Must be **Book Building** Process
    *   **75%** Mandatory allotment to **QIBs**
    *   If 75% not allotted -> **Refund**
    * Other conditions if OFS - Ensuring skin in the business
        - If seller holding above 50%
            - He can sell up to 20% of entity 
        - If seller holding less than 20% of entity
            - He can only sell up to 10% of the entity 

### 🧬 SR Equity Shares (Superior Rights)
*   Issuer Tech Intensive 🖥️
*   SR Shareholder Net Worth: Max **₹1000 Cr**
*   Voting Ratio: Min **2:1** | Max **10:1**
*   No IPO of SR shares (Lock-in until conversion)
* Only to promoters given 
* Only 1 class is allowed + It should be of same FV as others

## 💰 Promoters' Contribution & Lock-in
### 📥 Contribution (Reg 14)
*   **Minimum:** **20%** of Post-Issue Capital
*   *Alternative:* AIFs/FVCIs can contribute up to 10% to meet shortfall
*   **Condition:** Bring in **1 day prior** to opening

### 🔒 Lock-in Periods (Reg 16)
| Holding Type | Standard | If Majority Proceeds for CAPEX |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Min Contribution (20%)** | **18 Months** | **3 Years** |
| **Excess Contribution** | **6 Months** | **1 Year** |
| *Start Date* | Date of Allotment | Date of Allotment |

### 🔓 Exemptions
*   Transfers between Promoters
*   Transmission (Will/Succession)
*   *Note:* Lock-in period continues with transferee

## 📊 Allocation Ratios (Reg 32)
### The "Grid"
| Category | Profitability Route (6(1)) | QIB Route (6(2)) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **RII** (Retail) | Min **35%** | Max **10%** |
| **NII** (HNI) | Min **15%** | Min **15%** |
| **QIB** | Max **50%** | Min **75%** |

### 🧩 NII Sub-Categorization
*   **1/3rd:** Application **₹2L - ₹10L**
*   **2/3rd:** Application **> ₹10L**

### ⚓ Anchor Investors
*   Allocate up to **60% of QIB Portion**
*   Min App: **₹10 Cr** (Main Board) | ₹2 Cr (SME)
*   **Lock-in:**
    *   **50% shares:** 30 Days
    *   **50% shares:** 90 Days

## 📉 Pricing & Process
### 🏷️ Price Band (Reg 29)
*   Cap Price Range - 1.05 to 1.2 times the Floor Value
*   Announcement: **2 Working Days** before opening
*   **Face Value:** Disclosure Mandatory and Floor shall not be less than FV

### ⏱️ Timelines
*   **Open Period:** Min **3** | Max **10** Working Days
*   **Extension:** Min 3 days if Price Band revised
*   **Refund:** Within **4 Days** of closure (Interest @15% if delayed)
*   **Min Subscription:** **90%** of offer

### 🏦 General Corporate Purposes (GCP)
*   Max **25%** of Issue Size

## 🚀 Other Issue Types
### 🔄 Rights Issue
*   **Record Date:** Must announce
*   **Fast Track:** No Draft Offer Doc to SEBI if:
    *   Listed 3 years
    *   Avg Market Cap **₹250 Cr**
    *   95% Investor grievances resolved
    *   No Show-cause notices
*   **Min Sub:** 90%

### 🎁 Bonus Issue
*   **Source:** Free Reserves / Securities Premium / CRR
*   **Prohibited:** From Revaluation Reserves or in lieu of Dividend
*   **Checklist:**
    *   Authorized by AoA
    *   No default in debt/interest/statutory dues
    *   Partly paid shares made **fully paid**
*   **Time:** Implement within **15 days** (Board approval) or **2 months** (Shareholder approval)
*   *Once announced, cannot be withdrawn*

### 🤝 Preferential Issue (Pvt Placement)
*   **Pricing:** Higher of:
    *   Avg of 90 trading days (VWAP)
    *   Avg of 10 trading days (VWAP)
*   **Lock-in:**
    *   Promoters (20%): **18 Months**
    *   Promoters (Excess): **6 Months**
    *   Non-Promoters: **6 Months**
*   **Payment:** 100% upfront (Cash/Consideration)

### 🏢 QIP (Qualified Inst. Placement)
*   **Eligibility:** Listed 1 year, Special Resolution
*   **Gap:** Min **2 weeks** between two QIPs
*   **Pricing:** Floor price (avg 2 weeks). Max **5% discount** allowed.
*   **Tenure:** Convertible securities max 60 months
*   **Lock-in:** 1 Year (cannot sell on stock exchange)
### Warrants Issue
- Tenure max 18 months
- 25% of the total paid up should be received - during warrant issue
- Refund within 3 months if not issued
## 🚫 Ineligibility (Common Rules)
*   **Debarred** by SEBI
*   **Wilful Defaulter** / Fraudulent Borrower
*   **Fugitive Economic Offender**
*   Promoters/Directors associated with debarred company
