# SEBI SAST Regulations (Last Minute View) 🎯

## 1️⃣ Key Definitions

### Acquirer
- Person acquiring shares/voting rights/control
- **Directly or Indirectly**
- Includes Persons Acting in Concert (PAC)

### Target Company
- Listed company whose shares are being acquired
- *Exception:* IGP listed companies (special thresholds)

### Persons Acting in Concert (PAC) 🤝
- **Common Objective:** Acquire shares/voting rights/control
- **Deemed PAC Categories:**
  - Holding-Subsidiary-Same Management
  - Company + Directors + Management
  - Promoter Group Members
  - Immediate Relatives
  - Mutual Fund + Sponsor/Trustee/AMC
  - Merchant Banker + Client (Acquirer)
  - Investment Company + 10%+ Shareholders

### Control 🎛️
- Right to appoint **Majority Directors**
- Control Management/Policy Decisions
- Via shareholding/agreements/any manner
- *Exception:* Director/Officer ≠ Control by position alone

### Frequently Traded Shares 📊
- Traded Turnover ≥ **10% of Total Shares**
- Period: **12 months** preceding PA month
- Use: Weighted Average if share capital changes

## 2️⃣ Trigger Points & Open Offer Obligations 🛑

### Reg 3(1): Initial Acquisition Trigger
| **Threshold** | **Action Required** |
|---|---|
| ≥ **25%** Voting Rights | 📢 Mandatory Open Offer |
| *IGP Companies:* ≥ **49%** | 📢 Mandatory Open Offer |

**Formula:** Acquirer + PAC Holdings ≥ 25% → Open Offer

### Reg 3(2): Creeping Acquisition Trigger
| **Current Holding** | **Additional Acquisition** | **Action** |
|---|---|---|
| 25%-75% (Max Non-Public) | > **5%** in any FY | 📢 Open Offer |
| *Exception FY 2020-21:* | 5%-10% via Preferential Issue | ✅ Allowed |

**Key Points:**
- **Gross Acquisitions** counted (ignore intermittent fall)
- Fresh Issue: Post-Allotment % - Pre-Allotment % = Acquisition
- Cannot exceed Max Permissible Non-Public Shareholding

### Reg 3(3): Individual Shareholding Trigger
- Even if PAC aggregate unchanged
- Individual crosses threshold → Open Offer

### Reg 4: Acquisition of Control 🎛️
- **Irrespective of shareholding %**
- Acquire control → Mandatory Open Offer

### Reg 5: Indirect Acquisition
**Tests (if any > 80%):**
| **Parameter** | **Threshold** |
|---|---|
| Proportionate Net Asset Value | > 80% |
| Proportionate Sales Turnover | > 80% |
| Proportionate Market Cap | > 80% |

    - **If > 80%:** Treat as **Direct Acquisition**
    -**If 15%-80%:** Disclose per share value in Letter of Offer

## 3️⃣ Conditional Offers (Reg 19) 🎲

### Requirements
- Acquirer specifies **Minimum Acceptance Level**
- Agreement must have **Rescission Clause** if min not met

### Restrictions
- Cannot acquire during offer period (except under offer/agreement)
- If min not met → No shares acquired, agreement rescinded

### Competing Offer Impact
- 1st Offer unconditional → Competing offers cannot be conditional
- All conditional offers allowed if 1st is conditional

## 4️⃣ Disclosure Obligations 📄

### Reg 29: Acquisition/Disposal Disclosures
- **Disclosure To:**
    - All Stock Exchanges (shares listed)
    - Target Company (Registered Office)

- **Special Cases:**
    - **Encumbrance = Acquisition** (Pledge taken)
    - **Release = Disposal** (Pledge released)
    - *Exempt:* Banks/PFI/HFC/NBFC pledge for ordinary lending
- Disclosure Criteria
  - Initial: $\ge$ 5% shares/voting rights
  - Change: $\pm$ 2% (if holding $\ge$ 5%)
  - *IGP Listed*: $\ge$ 10% (Initial) / $\pm$ 5% (Change)
### Reg 31: Encumbrance Disclosures (Promoters)
| **Event** | **Timeline** | **Recipient** |
|---|---|---|
| Creation/Invocation/Release | **7 WD** | Stock Exchanges + Target Co |
| Annual Declaration (No Encumbrance) | FY end + **7 WD** | Stock Exchanges + Audit Committee |

*Exception:* Depository encumbrances exempt

### During Offer Period (Reg 18(6))
| **Acquisition Type** | **Timeline** |
|---|---|
| Any acquisition by Acquirer/PAC | **24 hours** to Stock Exchanges + TC |

**Blackout Period:** Cannot acquire/sell **T-3 WD to Tendering Close**

## 5️⃣ Obligations Summary 📑

### Directors of Target Company (Reg 24)
| **Restriction** | **Exception** |
|---|---|
| 🚫 No Acquirer/PAC Director Appointment | ✅ After 15 WD if 100% escrow in cash |
| 🚫 Conditional Offer: No appointment at all | - |
| 🚫 Competing Offers: No new directors | ✅ Vacancy (death): Postal ballot approval |
| 🚫 Existing Acquirer Director: No participation | Cannot vote/deliberate on offer matters |

### Acquirer (Reg 25)
- ✅ Ensure firm financial arrangements before PA
- ✅ Detailed Public Stmt/Letter Of Offer: True, fair, adequate, not misleading
- 🚫 Cannot sell TC shares during offer period
- ✅ If no alienation intent declared: **2-year bar** post-offer (unless special resolution)
- ✅ Joint & Several Liability (Acquirer + PAC)

### Target Company (Reg 26)
- ✅ Conduct business in **Ordinary Course** during offer period
- 🚫 **Without Special Resolution (Postal Ballot):**
  - Alienate material assets
  - Material borrowings (outside ordinary course)
  - Issue shares (voting rights)
  - Buy-back/capital structure changes
  - Material contracts (outside ordinary course)
  - Accelerate vesting (ESOPs, etc.)
- 🚫 No record date: **T-3 WD to Tendering Close**
- ✅ Provide shareholder list: Identified Date + **2 WD**
- ✅ Constitute **Independent Directors Committee**
- ✅ Committee Recommendation: **2 WD before Tendering** (same newspapers as PA)
- ✅ Facilitate share verification
- ✅ Equal treatment: All competing acquirers
- ✅ Register share transfers promptly

### Manager to Open Offer (Reg 27)
- ✅ Verify acquirer's ability + firm fund arrangements (before PA)
- ✅ Due diligence certificate with DLOF
- ✅ Ensure PA/DPS/LOF compliance
- ✅ Engage only SEBI-registered intermediaries
- 🚫 Cannot deal in TC shares during offer period
- ✅ File completion report: Tendering close + **15 WD**

## 1️⃣2️⃣ Quick Mnemonics & Formulas 🧮

### Trigger Memory: **"25-5-75"**
- **25%** = Initial Trigger
- **5%** = Creeping Acquisition (annual)
- **75%** = Max Non-Public Shareholding

### Disclosure Thresholds: **"5-2-7"**
- **5%** = Initial disclosure
- **2%** = Change disclosure
- **7 WD** = Encumbrance disclosure timeline

### Timeline Memory: **"5-5-12-10-10"**
- **5 WD:** DPS after PA
- **5 WD:** DLOF after DPS
- **12 WD:** Tendering starts (max after SEBI comments)
- **10 WD:** Tendering period
- **10 WD:** Payment after tendering

### Offer Size Memory: **"26-10"**
- **26%** = Mandatory Open Offer
- **10%** = Voluntary Open Offer
