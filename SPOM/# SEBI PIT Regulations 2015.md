# SEBI PIT Regulations 2015 🎯

## 📋 Key Definitions

### 🕵️ Insider
- **Connected Person** OR
- **In possession of/access to UPSI**
- 🔴 Onus: Prove NOT in possession

#### Connected Person
- **Direct Association** (6 months prior)
  - Director, Officer, Employee
  - Contractual/Fiduciary relationship
  - Frequent communication with officers
  - Access to UPSI (actual/expected)
- **Deemed Connected** (Rebuttable)
  - **Relatives** (spouse, parent, sibling, child)
  - Holding/Subsidiary/Associate Co.
  - Intermediaries (Sec 12 SEBI Act)
  - AMC, Trustees, Directors
  - Stock Exchange Officials
  - Bankers of company
  - Concern/Firm/HUF (>10% holding)
  - Sharing household/residence

### 🤐 UPSI (Unpublished Price Sensitive Information)
- **Definition:** Info NOT generally available + Materially affects price
    
    | **Category** | **Examples** |
    |---|---|
    | **Financial** | Results, Dividends, Fund Raising |
    | **Structural** | Capital structure change, M&A, Delisting |
    | **Personnel** | KMP changes (except superannuation), Auditor resignation |
    | **Ratings/Contracts** | Rating change (non-ESG), Order/Contract award/termination |
    | **Legal/Compliance** | Fraud, Default, Arrest of KMP/Promoter/Director |
    | **Insolvency** | Winding up, CIRP, Resolution plan |
    | **Forensic** | Forensic audit initiation/report |
    | **Regulatory** | Actions by authorities, Litigation outcomes |
    | **Guarantees** | Guarantee/Indemnity (not normal course) |
    | **Licenses** | License grant/withdrawal/suspension |

### 📊 Generally Available Info
- **Accessible** to public (non-discriminatory)
- 🚫 Excludes: Unverified media reports
- ✅ Stock Exchange website = Generally available

### 💼 Trading
- Subscribe, Redeem, Switch, Buy, Sell, Deal
- **Includes:** Pledging, Derivatives
- **Derivatives:** Traded value counted

## 🚫 Communication & Trading Restrictions

### Regulation 3: Communication of UPSI

#### ❌ Prohibition
- **No Insider** shall communicate/provide/allow access to UPSI
- **Exception:** Legitimate Purpose | Performance of Duties | Legal Obligation

#### ✅ Legitimate Purpose Policy
- Board to define in **Code of Fair Disclosure**
- **Examples:** Share with partners, lenders, customers, suppliers, bankers, legal advisors, auditors, consultants
- 🔴 NOT to evade regulations
- 🔔 **Recipient becomes Insider** → Confidentiality notice mandatory

#### 📁 Structured Digital Database (SDD)
- **Who Maintains:**
    - Listed Companies
    - Intermediaries/Fiduciaries handling UPSI

- **Contents:**
    - Nature of UPSI
    - Names of Sender + Receiver
    - PAN (or alternate ID)
    - Timestamp, Audit trails

- **Rules:**
    - ❌ NO outsourcing (incl. cloud servers)
    - **Entry deadline:** Within 2 calendar days (external UPSI)
    - **Retention:** 8 years post-transaction | Till investigation ends

#### 💰 Communication for Transactions
- **Takeover Scenario (Open Offer):**
    - Board approves (best interest)
    - UPSI can be shared

- **Non-Takeover Scenario:**
    - Board approves (best interest)
    - UPSI disseminated **2 trading days BEFORE** transaction
    - Must be adequate & fair disclosure

- **Confidentiality:**
    - Execute **NDA** with parties
    - Parties cannot trade while possessing UPSI

### Regulation 4: Trading with UPSI

#### 🛑 Core Prohibition
- **No Insider shall trade when in possession of UPSI**

- **Presumption:** Trade = Motivated by UPSI knowledge

#### ✅ Defenses (Prove Innocence)

| **Defense** | **Conditions** |
|---|---|
| **Off-Market Inter-Se Transfer** | Between insiders with SAME UPSI, Conscious trade, NOT via Reg 3(3), Report within 2 working days |
| **Block Deal Window** | Between parties with UPSI, Conscious trade, NOT via Reg 3(3) |
| **Statutory/Regulatory Obligation** | Bona fide transaction |
| **Stock Option Exercise** | Pre-determined price (per regulations) |
| **Non-Individual Insiders** | Decision-makers ≠ UPSI holders, Chinese Wall effective |
| **Trading Plan** | As per Regulation 5 |

#### ⚖️ Onus of Proof
- **Connected Persons:** Must prove NOT in possession
- **Others:** SEBI bears onus

## 📝 Trading Plans (Regulation 5)

### 🎯 Purpose
- Enable perpetual UPSI holders to trade compliantly
- Pre-decided trades BEFORE UPSI exists

### 📋 Plan Requirements

| **Parameter** | **Specification** |
|---|---|
| **Cool-off Period** | ≥ 120 calendar days from public disclosure (Earlier 6 months)|
| **No Overlap** | With another plan |
| **Per Trade Details** | Value/Quantity, Nature (buy/sell), Date/Period (≤5 trading days) |
| **Price Limit (Optional)** | Buy: Closing price to +20% / Sell: Closing price to -20% |
| **No Market Abuse** | Cannot manipulate UPSI release timing |

### ✅ Approval & Execution
- **Compliance Officer** reviews & approves within **2 trading days**
- Notify **Stock Exchanges** same day
- **Irrevocable:** No changes except:
  - Permanent incapacity
  - Bankruptcy
  - Operation of law
  
- **Non-Execution Protocol:**
    1. Insider intimates CO within 2 trading days (end of plan tenure)
    2. CO places before **Audit Committee** (next meeting)
    3. AC decides: Bona fide or not
    4. CO notifies Stock Exchange **same day**

- ### 🔓 Benefits
    - ❌ NO pre-clearance needed
    - ❌ NO trading window restrictions
    - ✅ Execute if price within limit (if set)

### ⚠️ Pre-Conditions for Commencement
- UPSI (at plan formulation) must be **generally available**
- If NOT available → Defer execution

## 📢 Disclosures by Insiders
### Generally
- Any person who is involved in the insider trading + immediate relatives 
- Not just equity market- also derivatives
- Maintain disclosure for 5 years

### 🔰 Initial Disclosure (Regulation 7.1)
- **WHO:** KMP, Director, Promoter, Promoter Group Member
- **WHEN:** Within **7 days** of appointment/becoming promoter
- **WHAT:** Holdings as on date
### 🔄 Continual Disclosure (Regulation 7.2)
- 
| **Trigger** | **Timeline** | **Threshold** |
|---|---|---|
| **Trading by Promoter/Promoter Group/Designated Person/Director** | Within **2 trading days** | Traded value > ₹10 Lakhs (per quarter) |
| **Company Notification** | Within **2 trading days** of receipt | To Stock Exchange |

### **Incremental Disclosure:** After prior disclosure, next disclosure when threshold crossed again
- **Includes:**
    - Immediate relatives
    - Persons for whom trade decisions taken
    - Derivatives (traded value)

### 🏢 Optional Disclosures
- Company may require **other connected persons** to disclose
- Frequency: As determined by company

## 📜 Codes of Conduct

### Code of Fair Disclosure (Regulation 8)
- **Applicable to:** Listed Companies

- **Content:**
    - Principles per Schedule A
    - Prompt UPSI disclosure (no selective)
    - Chief Investor Relations Officer
    - Response to queries/rumours
    - Analyst meetings: Share only public info
    - Transcripts on website
    - Need-to-know basis

- **Amendment:** Notify Stock Exchanges promptly

---

### Code of Conduct (Regulation 9)

#### 🏢 Listed Companies (Schedule B)
- **Formulated by:** CEO/MD (Board approval)

- **Covers:** Designated Persons + Immediate Relatives

- **Minimum Standards:**

- 
    | **Requirement** | **Details** |
    |---|---|
    | **Compliance Officer** | Reports to Board/Audit Committee (≥1/year) |
    | **Chinese Wall** | Need-to-know basis, Cross-wall process |
    | **Trading Window** | Closed when UPSI expected, Closed till 48 hrs post-results |
    | **Pre-Clearance** | Above Board-set threshold, Valid ≤7 trading days |
    | **Contra-Trade Ban** | ≥6 months (relaxation: CO discretion), Disgorge profits if violated |
    | **Sanctions** | Wage freeze, Suspension, Recovery → IPEF |
    | **Violation Reporting** | Promptly to Stock Exchange |
    | **Annual Disclosure** | Immediate relatives, Material financial relationship (≥25% income), Phone numbers, Education/Past employers (one-time) |

- **Exceptions (No Window Restriction):**
    - Off-market transfers, Block deals, Stock options
    - Rights issue, Open offer, Buyback
    - Pledge for bonafide purpose (pre-cleared)

#### 🏦 Intermediaries & Fiduciaries (Schedule C)
**Fiduciaries:** Auditors, Law firms, Analysts, Consultants, Banks

**Key Differences from Schedule B:**
- **Restricted List:** CO maintains (for pre-clearance)
- **Contra-Trade:** ≥6 months (listed co. securities), ≥2 months (MF units)
- Pre-clearance mandatory (above threshold)
- Report violations to Stock Exchange

## 💰 Designated Persons

### 🎯 Who are Designated Persons?
**Listed Companies:**
- Promoters (all)
- CEO + Employees (upto 2 levels below CEO)
- Material subsidiary employees (functional role/UPSI access)
- Support staff (IT, Secretarial with UPSI access)
- Employees designated by Board (functional role/UPSI access)

**Intermediaries/Fiduciaries:**
- As per role/function + UPSI access

## 🚨 Informant Incentive & Rewards (Chapter IIIA)

### 🕵️ Who can be Informant?
**Individual(s)** submitting Voluntary Info Disclosure (VID) on insider trading violation

**Original Information:**
- Independent knowledge/analysis
- NOT known to SEBI
- Specific, Credible, Timely (≤3 years from 1st alleged trade)
- NOT from judicial/govt reports/media (unless original source)
- NOT irrelevant/frivolous/vexatious

**Source:** Investor Protection & Education Fund (IPEF)

**SEBI discretion:** Can exempt from disqualifications

### 🛡️ Confidentiality & Protection
- **Confidentiality:**
    - Identity protected (RTI Act exemption)
    - Shared only: Legal proceedings, Regulatory agencies, Prosecutors
    - 7 days advance notice if evidence required

- **Protection from Retaliation:**
    - Code of Conduct must protect against discharge/demotion/harassment
    - Informant can approach courts
    - Employer liable: Penalty, Debarment, Criminal prosecution

- **No Amnesty:**
    - Informant NOT exempt from prosecution
    - Co-operation considered in penalty determination


## ⚖️ Penalties (Section 15G SEBI Act)

### 💸 Penalty Amount
- **₹10 Lakhs to ₹25 Crores**
- **OR**
- **3x Profits from Insider Trading**
- **(Whichever is HIGHER)**

### 🚨 Violations Covered
1. Trading on basis of UPSI (self/others)
2. Communicating UPSI (except ordinary business/legal)
3. Counseling/Procuring others to trade on UPSI
