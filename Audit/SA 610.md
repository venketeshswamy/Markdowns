# SA 610(Revised): Using the Work of Internal Auditors

## Objective
- Determine whether work of internal audit function can be used (Para 13a)
- Determine if work is adequate for audit purposes (Para 13b)
- Appropriately direct, supervise and review direct assistance work (Para 13c)

## Scope
- External auditor responsibilities when using internal auditors' work (Para 1)
- ⚠️ Does not apply if entity has no internal audit function (Para 2)
- ⚠️ Requirements for using function do not apply if (Para 3):
  - Responsibilities and activities are not relevant to the audit (Para 3a)
  - 🔗 Auditor does not expect to use function under SA 315 (Para 3b)
- Effective Date: Audits for periods beginning on or after April 1, 2016 (Para 12)

## Definitions
- 📌 Internal audit function: A function of an entity that performs assurance and consulting activities (Para 14a)
- 📌 Direct assistance: Use of internal auditors to perform audit procedures under external auditor's direction (Para 14b)

## Requirements
- Evaluating the Internal Audit Function
  - 🎯 Auditor shall evaluate objectivity, competence, and systematic approach (Para 15)
  - ⚠️ Auditor shall not use work if function lacks these (Para 16)
- Determining Nature and Extent of Work
  - 🎯 Consider nature and scope of performed/planned work (Para 17)
  - 🎯 Auditor shall make all significant judgments (Para 18)
  - ⚠️ Plan to use less work of function if (Para 18):
    - More judgment is involved in planning or evaluating (Para 18a)
    - Higher assessed risk of material misstatement at assertion level (Para 18b)
    - ⚠️ Lower objectivity or competence of internal audit function (Para 18c, d)
  - 🎯 Evaluate if aggregate use ensures sufficient external auditor involvement (Para 19)
- Using the Work of the Function
  - 🎯 Discuss planned use of work with the function (Para 21)
  - 🎯 Read internal audit reports to understand procedures and findings (Para 22)
  - 🎯 Perform sufficient procedures to determine adequacy of work (Para 23)
  - 🎯 Procedures shall be responsive to judgments and risks (Para 24)
  - 🎯 Shall include reperformance of some of the work (Para 24)
- Direct Assistance (DA)
  - ⚠️ Auditor may be prohibited by law from obtaining DA (Para 26)
  - 🎯 Evaluate threats to objectivity and level of competence (Para 27)
  - ⚠️ Shall not use DA if significant objectivity threats exist (Para 28a)
  - ⚠️ Shall not use DA if auditor lacks sufficient competence (Para 28b)
  - ⚠️ Shall not use DA for significant judgments (Para 30a)
  - ⚠️ Shall not use DA for higher risk with non-limited judgment (Para 30b)
  - ⚠️ Shall not use DA for work IAF already reported (Para 30c)
  - ⚠️ Shall not use DA for SA 610 related decisions (Para 30d)
- Using Direct Assistance
  - 🎯 Obtain written agreement from authorized entity representative (Para 33a)
  - 🎯 Obtain written agreement from internal auditors regarding confidentiality (Para 33b)
  - 🎯 Direct, supervise and review DA in accordance with SA 220 (Para 34)
  - 🎯 Check back to underlying evidence for some DA work (Para 34b)

## Application Guidance
- IAF Definition (A1-A4)
  - IAF may be outsourced to third-party service provider (A2)
  - ⚠️ Monitoring by owner-manager not equivalent to IAF (A3)
- Evaluating IAF (A5-A14)
  - Objectivity and competence viewed as a continuum (A9)
  - ⚠️ Competence cannot compensate for lack of objectivity (A9)
  - ⚠️ Objectivity cannot compensate for lack of competence (A9)
  - ⚠️ Self-review threat if external auditor provides IAF services (A14)
- Using Work (A24-A30)
  - Coordination includes timing, coverage, and materiality levels (A24)
  - Reperformance provides more persuasive evidence than inquiries (A30)
- Direct Assistance (A31-A41)
  - ⚠️ Excessive use of DA may affect independence perceptions (A39)
  - Review includes sufficiency and appropriateness of obtained evidence (A41)

## Key Nuances and Exceptions
- ⚠️ Para 5/26: Law or regulation may prohibit or restrict use of IAF (Para 5, 26)
- ⚠️ Para 11: External auditor has sole responsibility for audit opinion (Para 11)
- ⚠️ Para 16: Non-use mandatory if organizational status doesn't support objectivity (Para 16a)
- ⚠️ Para 16: Non-use mandatory if function lacks sufficient competence (Para 16b)
- ⚠️ Para 16: Non-use mandatory if systematic approach is missing (Para 16c)
- ⚠️ Para 28: Specific individual internal auditors excluded if objectivity threats exist (Para 28)

## ICAI Listed Examples
- Para A1 — Activities of IAF
  - 📋 Governance process assessment (ethics, performance)
  - 📋 Risk management (identifying exposures, fraud detection)
  - 📋 Evaluation of internal control (reviewing/recommending improvements)
  - 📋 Examination of financial and operating information
  - 📋 Review of operating activities (economy, efficiency)
  - 📋 Review of compliance with laws/regulations
- Para A7 — Factors affecting Objectivity
  - 📋 Organizational status supporting bias-free judgments
  - 📋 Reporting to TCWG or authorized officer
  - 📋 Direct access to TCWG
  - 📋 Freedom from conflicting responsibilities (managerial/operational)
  - 📋 TCWG oversight of employment/remuneration decisions
  - 📋 Constraints/restrictions from management
  - 📋 Membership in professional bodies (standards compliance)
- Para A8 — Factors affecting Competence
  - 📋 Adequate and appropriate resources relative to size
  - 📋 Established policies for hiring/training/assigning
  - 📋 Technical training and proficiency (designations/experience)
  - 📋 Knowledge of financial reporting framework
  - 📋 Industry-specific knowledge
- Para A16 — Work IAF can perform
  - 📋 Testing operating effectiveness of controls
  - 📋 Substantive procedures with limited judgment
  - 📋 Observations of inventory counts
  - 📋 Tracing transactions through information systems
  - 📋 Testing compliance with regulatory requirements
  - 📋 Audits of non-significant component subsidiaries (🔗 SA 600)
- Para A19 — Significant judgments for external auditor
  - 📋 Assessing risks of material misstatement
  - 📋 Evaluating sufficiency of tests performed
  - 📋 Evaluating appropriateness of going concern assumption
  - 📋 Evaluating significant accounting estimates
  - 📋 Evaluating adequacy of financial statement disclosures
- Para A36 — Tasks inappropriate for DA
  - 📋 Discussion of fraud risks
  - 📋 Determination of unannounced audit procedures

## Logical Flow Chains
- Decision to use IAF Work (Para 15)
  - leads to: Evaluate Objectivity, Competence, Approach (Para 15)
    - if all met: Determine Nature and Extent (Para 17)
    - ⚠️ unless: Quality risks too significant (Para 16)
- Decision to use DA (Para 27)
  - leads to: Evaluate individual's objectivity and competence (Para 27)
    - if met: Obtain written agreements (Para 33)
    - then: Assign specific non-judgmental tasks (Para 29-30)
