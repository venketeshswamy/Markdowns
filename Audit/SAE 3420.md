# SAE 3420: Assurance Engagements to Report on Compilation of Pro Forma Financial Information Included in a Prospectus

## Objective
- 🎯 To obtain reasonable assurance about whether compiled properly (Para 10a)
  - Compiled in all material respects (Para 10a)
  - On the basis of the applicable criteria (Para 10a)
- 🎯 To report in accordance with practitioner findings (Para 10b)

## Scope
- Applies to reasonable assurance compilation engagements (Para 1)
  - Where required by securities law or regulation (Para 1)
  - Or generally accepted practice in jurisdiction (Para 1)
- Practitioner has no responsibility to compile (Para 2)
  - Responsibility rests with the responsible party (Para 2)
- Does NOT deal with non-assurance compilations (Para 3)
- Effective for reports dated on/after April 1, 2016 (Para 9)

## Definitions
- 📌 Applicable criteria (Para 11a): The criteria used by the responsible party when compiling the pro forma financial information.
- 📌 Pro forma adjustments (Para 11b): Adjustments to unadjusted financial information illustrating impact of significant event and necessary for consistent basis.
- 📌 Pro forma financial information (Para 11c): Financial information shown together with adjustments to illustrate the impact of an event.
- 📌 Prospectus (Para 11d): A document issued pursuant to legal or regulatory requirements relating to securities.
- 📌 Published financial information (Para 11e): Financial information of the entity or acquiree/divestee made available publicly.
- 📌 Unadjusted financial information (Para 11f): Financial information of the entity to which pro forma adjustments are applied.

## Requirements
- Framework & Acceptance (Para 12-13)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall not represent compliance (Para 12)
    - ⚠️ Unless complied with both SAE and Assurance Framework (Para 12)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall determine capabilities and competence (Para 13a)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall determine criteria are suitable (Para 13b)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall evaluate prescribed opinion wording (Para 13c)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall consider modified source opinions (Para 13d)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall evaluate if entity history unaudited (Para 13e)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall evaluate if acquiree history unaudited (Para 13f)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall obtain responsible party agreement (Para 13g)
- Planning & Performing (Para 14-17)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall assess criteria minimums (Para 14)
    - Extracted from appropriate source (Para 14a)
    - Directly attributable and factually supportable (Para 14b)
    - Consistent with applicable framework (Para 14b)
    - Appropriate presentation and disclosures made (Para 14c)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall assess if criteria consistent with law (Para 15a)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall assess if unlikely to mislead (Para 15b)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall consider materiality (Para 16)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall obtain understanding of entity (Para 17)
- Evidence on Source Appropriateness (Para 18-20)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall determine extraction source appropriateness (Para 18)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall perform procedures if source unaudited (Para 19)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall determine appropriate extraction (Para 20)
- Evidence on Adjustments & Calculations (Para 21-25)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall determine all adjustments identified (Para 21)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall determine adjustments meet criteria (Para 22)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall evaluate modified source consequences (Para 23)
    - Evaluate consequence on compilation (Para 23a)
    - Evaluate further appropriate action (Para 23b)
    - Evaluate effect on practitioner report (Para 23c)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall discuss inappropriate elements (Para 24)
    - If unable to agree, evaluate further action (Para 24)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall determine arithmetical accuracy (Para 25)
- Evaluating Presentation (Para 26-27)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall evaluate overall presentation (Para 26)
    - Clearly labeled to distinguish from historical info (Para 26a)
    - Illustrates impact without being misleading (Para 26b)
    - Appropriate disclosures provided (Para 26c)
    - Significant subsequent events considered (Para 26d)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall read other information in prospectus (Para 27)
    - Discuss material inconsistencies with responsible party (Para 27)
- Representations & Forming Opinion (Para 28-30)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall request written representations (Para 28)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall form opinion on compilation (Para 29)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall conclude if free from omissions (Para 30)
- Form of Opinion (Para 31-34)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall express unmodified opinion if compiled properly (Para 31)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall discuss necessary changes if modified (Para 32)
    - ⚠️ If law precludes publishing modified opinion (Para 32)
      - Withdraw from engagement (Para 32a)
      - Or consider seeking legal advice (Para 32b)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall apply modified opinion requirements (Para 33)
    - ⚠️ Where law allows publication of modified opinion (Para 33)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall include Emphasis of Matter if fundamental (Para 34)
    - ⚠️ Provided sufficient appropriate evidence matter doesn't affect compilation (Para 34)
- Preparing Assurance Report (Para 35)
  - 🎯 The practitioner shall include basic report elements (Para 35)

## Application Guidance
- Scope & Purpose (A1-A6)
  - Does not deal with pro forma in standard financial statements (A1)
  - Notes often disclose basis and assumptions (A2)
  - Describes "properly compiled" in all material respects (A6)
- Definitions Guidance (A7-A9)
  - Responsible party develops criteria if established ones missing (A7)
  - Criteria suitable if they meet para 14 benchmarks (A8)
- Engagement Acceptance (A10-A12)
  - Assumes practitioner expects to obtain needed information (A11)
  - Law may prescribe terms in sufficient detail (A12)
- Assessing Criteria Suitability (A13-A16)
  - Directly attributable excludes future dependent events (A13)
  - Accounting policies must align with reporting framework (A15)
- Materiality (A17-A18)
  - Depends on size and nature of omission or misapplication (A17)
- Understanding the Compilation (A19-A26)
  - Acquiree may be separately identifiable unincorporated operation (A22)
  - Previous audits reduce need for new understanding (A23)
- Appropriateness of Source (A27-A31)
  - Source permitted by exchange or normal market custom (A27)
  - Need for understanding remains if source audited by another (A28)
  - Seldom permitted if historical info never audited (A31)
- Appropriateness of Adjustments (A32-A38)
  - Divestee info derived from consolidated source (A33)
  - Acquiree info may be audited by another practitioner (A36)
- Modified Opinions & Associations (A39-A44)
  - Not all modified source opinions affect the pro forma compilation (A39)
  - Must avoid association with misleading information (A41)
  - Not required to audit source for subsequent events (A43)
- Opinion & Report (A45-A57)
  - Law may require opinion on further matters (A46-A47)
  - Practitioner may agree addressee with entity (A52)
  - Wording may be prescribed by jurisdiction law (A54-A56)

## Key Nuances and Exceptions
- ⚠️ Para 12: Cannot represent compliance unless both SAE and Framework met.
- ⚠️ Para 23: If source has modified opinion, evaluate if law permits use.
- ⚠️ Para 32: If law precludes publishing modified opinion, must withdraw or seek legal advice.
- ⚠️ Para 34: Emphasis of matter requires evidence that compilation itself is unaffected.
- ⚠️ A43: Not required to identify subsequent events for the unadjusted source.

## Logical Flow Chains
- Source Extraction Path (Para 18-20)
  - Evaluate Unadjusted FI Source (Para 18)
    - if audited: check for modified opinion (Para 23)
    - if unaudited: perform procedures on source (Para 19)
      - leads to: confirm appropriateness of extraction (Para 20)
- Pro Forma Compilation Path (Para 21-26)
  - Identify Adjustments (Para 21)
    - leads to: Assess Adjustments Appropriateness (Para 22)
      - if directly attributable: accept (Para 22a)
      - and if factually supportable: accept (Para 22b)
      - and if consistent with framework: accept (Para 22c)
        - leads to: Check Mathematical Accuracy (Para 25)
          - leads to: Evaluate Presentation (Para 26)
