# SA 800 (Revised): Special Considerations—Audits of Financial Statements Prepared in Accordance with Special Purpose Frameworks

## Objective
- 🎯 The auditor shall address special considerations (Para 5)
  - Acceptance of the engagement (Para 5a)
  - Planning and performance of that engagement (Para 5b)
  - Forming an opinion and reporting (Para 5c)

## Scope
- Applies to: SAs 100-700 series application (Para 1)
- Context: Complete set of financial statements (Para 2)
- Does NOT apply to: Single statements or elements (Para 2)
  - 🔗 See SA 805 (Para 2)
- Does NOT override: Requirements of other SAs (Para 3)
- Effective from: April 1, 2024 (Para 4)

## Definitions
- Special purpose financial statements
  - 📌 Financial statements prepared in accordance with a special purpose framework. (Para 6a)
- Special purpose framework
  - 📌 A financial reporting framework designed to meet the financial information needs of specific users. The financial reporting framework may be a fair presentation framework or a compliance framework. (Para 6b)
- Financial statements
  - 📌 "a complete set of special purpose financial statements" including the related notes. (Para 7)

## Requirements
- Engagement Acceptance
  - 🎯 The auditor shall obtain an understanding of: (Para 8)
    - Purpose for which statements are prepared (Para 8a)
    - The intended users (Para 8b)
    - Steps by management to determine framework acceptability (Para 8c)
- Planning and Performing
  - 🎯 The auditor shall determine if SAs require special consideration (Para 9)
  - 🎯 The auditor shall obtain understanding of significant contract interpretations (Para 10)
    - ⚠️ Exception: Significant only if alternative creates material difference (Para 10)
- Forming Opinion and Reporting
  - 🎯 The auditor shall apply SA 700(Revised) requirements (Para 11)
  - 🎯 The auditor shall evaluate if statements describe the framework (Para 12)
  - 🎯 The auditor shall evaluate if statements describe significant interpretations (Para 12)
  - 🎯 The auditor's report shall describe purpose and intended users (Para 13a)
  - 🎯 Management responsibility explanation shall reference framework acceptability (Para 13b)
    - ⚠️ If management has a choice of financial reporting frameworks (Para 13b)
  - 🎯 The auditor's report shall include an Emphasis of Matter paragraph (Para 14)
    - Alerting users to the special purpose framework (Para 14)
    - Alerting that statements may not suit another purpose (Para 14)

## Application Guidance
- Explains Para 6 (Frameworks)
  - May not comply with all framework requirements (Para A2)
  - Inappropriate to imply full compliance in description (Para A2)
  - May lack fair presentation even if based on one (Para A3)
  - May be the only financial statements entity prepares (Para A4)
- Explains Para 8 (Acceptance)
  - Intended user needs are key for acceptability (Para A5)
  - Authorized organization frameworks presumed acceptable if transparent (Para A6)
  - Regulator provisions presumed acceptable absent contrary indications (Para A6)
  - Determine conflicts if supplemented by law or regulation (Para A7)
  - Contract frameworks acceptability judged by SA 210 attributes (Para A8)
- Explains Para 9 (Planning)
  - Comply with SAs unless not relevant or conditional (Para A9)
  - Materiality judgments based on intended user needs (Para A10)
  - Management threshold does not relieve SA 320 materiality requirement (Para A11)
  - Governance communication modified if all manage entity (Para A12)
- Explains Para 11-14 (Reporting)
  - Going concern description adapted based on framework (Para A15)
  - Key Audit Matters apply only if required by law (Para A16)
  - Owner reports considered annual reports for SA 720 (Para A17)
  - Auditor report shall be signed with specific MRN and FRN (Para A18)
  - May refer to general purpose FS report via Other Matter (Para A19)
  - EOM alerts users if statements placed on public record (Para A20)
  - May restrict distribution or use of auditor's report (Para A21)

## Key Nuances and Exceptions
- ⚠️ Para 10: Contract interpretation significance
  - If another reasonable interpretation creates material difference: Significant
  - If no material difference: Not significant
- ⚠️ Para 13b: Management responsibility reference
  - If management has framework choice: Mention responsibility for acceptability
  - If no choice: No specific mention required
- ⚠️ Para A11: Materiality exception
  - Management agreed threshold does not waive SA 320 auditor duty
- ⚠️ Para A16: Key Audit Matters applicability
  - SA 701 applies only if required by law or regulation
- ⚠️ ISA Modification: Tax basis framework
  - Tax basis FS for Indian income tax are general purpose FS

## Logical Flow Chains
- Acceptability of Framework Assessment (Para 8)
  - Identify intended users and purpose (Para 8)
    - leads to: Assessing framework source (Para A6)
      - if Authorized setter or Regulator: Presumed acceptable (Para A6)
      - if Contract or Other: Apply SA 210 attributes (Para A8)
        - requires: Professional judgment on attribute importance (Para A8)
- Addressing Management Misstatement Thresholds (Para A11)
  - Management agrees on a misstatement threshold with users (Para A11)
    - leads to: Auditor performing risk assessment
      - ⚠️ unless: Auditor still applies SA 320 materiality independently (Para A11)
