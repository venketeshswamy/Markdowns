# SA 720 (Revised): The Auditor’s Responsibilities Relating to Other Information

## Objective
- 🎯 Consider material inconsistency between other information and financial statements (Para 11a)
- 🎯 Consider material inconsistency between other information and auditor knowledge (Para 11b)
- 🎯 Respond appropriately when material inconsistencies or misstatements appear to exist (Para 11c)
- 🎯 Report in accordance with this SA (Para 11d)

## Scope
- Applies to: Other information included in entity annual report (Para 1)
- Does NOT apply to: Preliminary announcements of financial information (Para 7a)
- Does NOT apply to: Securities offering documents including prospectuses (Para 7b)
- Effective from: Audits for periods beginning on or after April 1, 2018 (Para 10)

## Definitions
- 📌 Annual report (Para 12a): A document, or combination of documents, prepared typically on an annual basis by management or those charged with governance in accordance with law, regulation or custom, the purpose of which is to provide owners (or similar stakeholders) with information on the entity’s operations and the entity’s financial results and financial position as set out in the financial statements.
- 📌 Misstatement of the other information (Para 12b): A misstatement of the other information exists when the other information is incorrectly stated or otherwise misleading (including because it omits or obscures information necessary for a proper understanding of a matter disclosed in the other information).
- 📌 Other information (Para 12c): Financial or non-financial information (other than financial statements and the auditor’s report thereon) included in an entity’s annual report.

## Requirements
- Obtaining the Other Information
  - 🎯 The auditor shall determine document comprising the annual report (Para 13a)
  - 🎯 The auditor shall make arrangements to obtain final version prior (Para 13b)
  - ⚠️ If unavailable before report date request written representation (Para 13c)
- Reading and Considering the Other Information
  - 🎯 The auditor shall read the other information (Para 14)
  - 🎯 The auditor shall consider material inconsistency with financial statements (Para 14a)
    - 🎯 The auditor shall compare selected amounts or other items (Para 14a)
  - 🎯 The auditor shall consider material inconsistency with auditor knowledge (Para 14b)
  - 🎯 The auditor shall remain alert for indications of material misstatement (Para 15)
- Responding to Apparent Inconsistencies or Misstatements
  - ⚠️ If material inconsistency appears exist discuss with management (Para 16)
  - 🎯 The auditor shall perform procedures to conclude if misstatement exists (Para 16)
- Responding to Concluded Misstatements in Other Information
  - 🎯 The auditor shall request management to correct other information (Para 17)
  - ⚠️ If management refuses correction communicate with those charged governance (Para 17b)
  - ⚠️ If uncorrected and obtained prior to auditor report date (Para 18)
    - 🎯 The auditor shall take appropriate action including (Para 18)
    - Consider implications for auditor report (Para 18a)
    - ⚠️ Withdraw from engagement where possible under law (Para 18b)
  - ⚠️ If uncorrected and obtained after auditor report date (Para 19)
    - 🎯 The auditor shall take appropriate action considering legal rights (Para 19b)
- Responding to Financial Statement Misstatement
  - ⚠️ If financial statements misstated or auditor understanding needs update (Para 20)
    - 🎯 The auditor shall respond appropriately per other SAs (Para 20)
- Reporting
  - 🎯 The auditor shall include separate section heading Other Information (Para 21)
  - ⚠️ If audit of listed entity and obtained or expects information (Para 21a)
  - ⚠️ If audit of unlisted corporate entity and obtained information (Para 21b)
  - 🎯 The section shall include statement management is responsible (Para 22a)
  - 🎯 The section shall identify other information obtained (Para 22b)
  - 🎯 The section shall state auditor opinion does not cover it (Para 22c)
  - 🎯 The section shall describe auditor responsibilities regarding other information (Para 22d)
  - ⚠️ If expressing qualified or adverse opinion consider implications (Para 23)
  - ⚠️ If law prescribes layout refer to SAs only if conditions met (Para 24)
- Documentation
  - 🎯 The auditor shall include documentation of procedures performed (Para 25a)
  - 🎯 The auditor shall include final version of other information (Para 25b)

## Application Guidance
- Explains Para 12 (Definitions)
  - Annual report content defined by law regulation or custom (A1)
  - Annual report typically prepared on annual basis (A2)
  - May be single document or combination of documents (A3)
  - Printed or electronic forms meet annual report definition (A4)
  - Different from specific stakeholder or regulatory standalone reports (A5)
  - Misstatement includes omitting or obscuring necessary information (A6)
  - Materiality context is common information needs of users (A7)
  - Framework required specific disclosures form part of financial statements (A9)
  - XBRL tags do not represent other information (A10)
- Explains Para 13 (Obtaining Info)
  - Timing and purpose relevant to determine annual report components (A11)
  - Translated reports or multiple jurisdiction reports need consideration (A12)
  - Auditor communicates expectations for obtaining final version timely (A13)
  - Final version is one approved by those charged with governance (A15)
  - Auditor not precluded from issuing report if info missing (A20)
  - Not required to update SA 560 procedures for late info (A21)
  - May request other written representations to support audit (A22)
- Explains Para 14-15 (Reading and Considering)
  - Maintain professional skepticism regarding overly optimistic management plans (A23)
  - Partner responsible for direction and supervision of engagement team (A24)
  - Not required to compare all amounts or items (A26)
  - Selecting items to compare requires professional judgment (A27)
  - Procedures nature and extent require professional judgment (A28)
  - Auditor knowledge includes entity understanding per SA 315 (A31)
  - Knowledge may include prospective matters like future cash flows (A32)
  - May focus on matters of sufficient importance for materiality (A33)
  - Recollection of audit evidence may be sufficient for consideration (A34)
  - May refer to audit documentation or engagement team members (A35)
  - Remaining alert assists complying with relevant ethical requirements (A38)
- Explains Para 16 (Apparent Inconsistencies)
  - Discussion may include requesting support for management statements (A39)
  - More difficult to challenge management on judgment matters (A41)
  - Nature and extent of further procedures are professional judgment (A42)
  - May request management consult qualified third party expert (A43)
- Explains Para 17-19 (Concluded Misstatements)
  - Refusal to correct may raise doubt about management integrity (A44)
  - Disclaimer of opinion possible if integrity severely doubted (A45)
  - Withdrawal possible if representations reliability called into question (A46)
  - Withdrawal may not be possible for government entities (A47)
  - If obtained after report date verify correction was made (A48)
  - If uncorrected seek legal advice regarding legal rights obligations (A49)
  - May provide amended auditor report to management and users (A50)
- Explains Para 20 (FS Misstatement)
  - May indicate need to revise auditor risk assessment (A51)
- Explains Para 21-24 (Reporting)
  - Unlisted entity expected info identification provides additional transparency (A52)
  - Qualified adverse opinion may not impact other information statement (A54)
  - Scope limitation modifies statement referring to inability to consider (A56)
  - Disclaimer of opinion omits other information reporting section entirely (A58)

## Key Nuances and Exceptions
- ⚠️ Para 13c: If information unavailable prior to report date — Request written representation
- ⚠️ Para 16: If inconsistency appears to exist — Discuss with management and perform procedures
- ⚠️ Para 17b: If management refuses to correct — Communicate with those charged with governance
- ⚠️ Para 18: If uncorrected and obtained prior to report date — Consider auditor report implications or withdraw
- ⚠️ Para 19: If uncorrected and obtained after report date — Take appropriate action considering legal rights
- ⚠️ Para 21b (India Modification): Unlisted non-corporate entities — Reporting requirements of SA 720 do not apply
- ⚠️ Para 23: If qualified or adverse opinion expressed — Consider implications for other information statement
- ⚠️ Para A47: Government entities — Withdrawal may not be possible, issue report to legislature
- ⚠️ Para A58: If disclaimer of opinion expressed — Do not include other information section

## Logical Flow Chains
- Reading and Responding Process (Para 14-20)
  - Read other information and consider inconsistencies (Para 14)
    - leads to: Identify apparent inconsistency or misstatement (Para 16)
      - if true: Discuss with management and perform procedures (Para 16)
        - leads to: Conclude material misstatement exists (Para 17)
          - requires: Request management to correct (Para 17)
            - if refused: Communicate with those charged with governance (Para 17b)
              - if uncorrected before report: Modify report or withdraw (Para 18)
              - if uncorrected after report: Seek legal advice take action (Para 19)
