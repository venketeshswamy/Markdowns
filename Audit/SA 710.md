# SA 710: Comparative Information - Corresponding Figures and Comparative Financial Statements

## Objective
- 🎯 To obtain sufficient appropriate audit evidence (Para 5a)
  - Whether comparative information is presented appropriately (Para 5a)
  - In all material respects per financial reporting framework (Para 5a)
- 🎯 To report in accordance with reporting responsibilities (Para 5b)

## Scope
- Applies to auditor responsibilities regarding comparative information (Para 1)
- 🔗 SA 510 applies if prior period unaudited or predecessor audited (Para 1)
- Depends on applicable financial reporting framework requirements (Para 2)
- Two broad reporting approaches exist (Para 2)
  - Corresponding figures approach (Para 3a)
  - Comparative financial statements approach (Para 3b)
- Effective for periods beginning on or after April 1, 2011 (Para 4)

## Definitions
- 📌 Comparative information (Para 6a): The amounts and disclosures included in the financial statements in respect of one or more prior periods in accordance with the applicable financial reporting framework.
- 📌 Corresponding figures (Para 6b): Comparative information where amounts and other disclosures for the prior period are included as an integral part of the current period financial statements, and are intended to be read only in relation to the amounts and other disclosures relating to the current period (referred to as “current period figures”). The level of detail presented in the corresponding amounts and disclosures is dictated primarily by its relevance to the current period figures.
- 📌 Comparative financial statements (Para 6c): Comparative information where amounts and other disclosures for the prior period are included for comparison with the financial statements of the current period but, if audited, are referred to in the auditor’s opinion. The level of information included in those comparative financial statements is comparable with that of the financial statements of the current period.

## Requirements
- Audit Procedures
  - 🎯 Auditor shall determine if required comparatives are included (Para 7)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall evaluate if information agrees with prior amounts (Para 7a)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall evaluate if accounting policies are consistent (Para 7b)
  - ⚠️ If aware of possible material misstatement in comparatives (Para 8)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall perform necessary additional audit procedures (Para 8)
    - 🔗 🎯 Auditor shall also follow SA 560 if prior audited (Para 8)
  - 🎯 Auditor shall request written representations for all periods (Para 9)
    - 🔗 🎯 As required by SA 580 (Para 9)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall obtain specific representation for disclosed prior items (Para 9)
- Corresponding Figures
  - 🎯 Auditor opinion shall not refer to corresponding figures (Para 10)
    - ⚠️ Except in circumstances described in paras 11, 12, 14 (Para 10)
  - ⚠️ If prior period report modified and matter unresolved (Para 11)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall modify opinion on current financial statements (Para 11)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall refer to both periods' figures if material (Para 11a)
    - 🎯 Or explain effects on comparability of figures (Para 11b)
  - ⚠️ If prior unmodified financial statements contain material misstatement (Para 12)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall verify if misstatement is properly dealt with (Para 12)
    - ⚠️ If not dealt with properly (Para 12)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall express qualified or adverse opinion (Para 12)
  - ⚠️ If prior period audited by predecessor auditor (Para 13)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall state in Other Matter paragraph (Para 13)
      - Prior financial statements audited by predecessor (Para 13a)
      - Type of opinion expressed and reasons if modified (Para 13b)
      - Date of predecessor auditor report (Para 13c)
  - ⚠️ If prior period financial statements were not audited (Para 14)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall state corresponding figures are unaudited (Para 14)
    - 🔗 🎯 Statement does not relieve auditor of SA 510 requirements (Para 14)
- Comparative Financial Statements
  - 🎯 Auditor opinion shall refer to each presented period (Para 15)
  - ⚠️ If opinion on prior period differs from previous opinion (Para 16)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall disclose substantive reasons for different opinion (Para 16)
    - 🔗 🎯 Disclosure in Other Matter paragraph per SA 706 (Para 16)
  - ⚠️ If prior period audited by predecessor auditor (Para 17)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall state in Other Matter paragraph (Para 17)
      - Prior financial statements audited by predecessor (Para 17a)
      - Type of opinion expressed and reasons if modified (Para 17b)
      - Date of predecessor auditor report (Para 17c)
    - ⚠️ Unless predecessor report is revised with financial statements (Para 17)
  - ⚠️ If misstatement affects previously unmodified predecessor report (Para 18)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall communicate to management and TCWG (Para 18)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall request predecessor auditor be informed (Para 18)
    - ⚠️ If predecessor agrees to issue new report on amended statements (Para 18)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall report only on current period (Para 18)
  - ⚠️ If prior period financial statements were not audited (Para 19)
    - 🎯 Auditor shall state comparative financial statements are unaudited (Para 19)
    - 🔗 🎯 Statement does not relieve auditor of SA 510 requirements (Para 19)

## Application Guidance
- Explains Audit Procedures
  - Written reps requested for all periods for comparative statements (A1)
  - Written reps requested for current period for corresponding figures (A1)
  - Management reaffirms previous written representations remain appropriate (A1)
- Explains Corresponding Figures
  - Opinion applies to current period financial statements as a whole (A2)
  - Opinion need not refer to resolved prior modifications (A3)
  - Unresolved matters may affect current comparability requiring modification (A4)
  - May include Emphasis of Matter if misstatement appropriately disclosed (A6)
  - 🎯 Auditor should request management disclose unaudited status on face (A7a)
  - 🔗 Unable to obtain opening balance evidence requires SA 705 modification (A8)
  - 🔗 Significant opening balance difficulty may be SA 701 Key Audit Matter (A8)
- Explains Comparative Financial Statements
  - Auditor may express different opinions for different periods presented (A9)
  - Opinion may differ if aware of new circumstances during audit (A11)
  - ⚠️ If predecessor unable or unwilling to revise report (A12)
    - Other Matter paragraph may indicate predecessor reported before amendment (A12)
    - Auditor may opine on appropriateness of amendment adjustments (A12)
  - 🔗 Unable to obtain opening balance evidence requires SA 705 modification (A13)
  - 🔗 Significant opening balance difficulty may be SA 701 Key Audit Matter (A13)
- Modifications vis-a-vis ISA 710
  - Restatement requirement replaced with AS 5 disclosure requirement (Mod 1)
  - Term reissue replaced with revised for Indian scenario (Mod 2)

## Key Nuances and Exceptions
- ⚠️ Para 10: Auditor opinion shall not refer to corresponding figures
  - If condition met: General rule applies
  - If condition not met: Except for unresolved modifications or misstatements
- ⚠️ Para 11: Prior period modified opinion unresolved
  - If material to current: Refer to both periods' figures
  - If affects comparability: Explain effects on comparability
- ⚠️ Para 12: Material misstatement in unmodified prior financial statements
  - If dealt with properly: Unmodified opinion on current period
  - If not dealt with properly: Qualified or adverse opinion modified for figures
- ⚠️ Para 17: Predecessor audited prior period comparative statements
  - If predecessor report revised: Do not reference predecessor in Other Matter
  - If predecessor report not revised: Reference predecessor in Other Matter

## Logical Flow Chains
- Prior Period Misstatement Discovery
  - Auditor aware of possible material misstatement in comparatives (Para 8)
    - leads to: Perform additional audit procedures (Para 8)
      - if material misstatement exists in unmodified prior statements: Verify framework compliance (Para 12)
        - if not properly dealt with: Express qualified or adverse opinion (Para 12)
- Predecessor Auditor Reporting Process
  - Prior period financial statements audited by predecessor (Para 18)
    - leads to: Auditor concludes material misstatement exists (Para 18)
      - leads to: Communicate to management and TCWG (Para 18)
        - leads to: Request predecessor be informed (Para 18)
          - if predecessor issues new report: Report only on current period (Para 18)
          - ⚠️ unless: Predecessor unwilling to revise (A12)
            - leads to: Indicate predecessor reported before amendment in Other Matter (A12)
