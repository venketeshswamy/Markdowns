# SA 560: Subsequent Events

## Objective
- 🎯 Obtain sufficient appropriate audit evidence (Para 4a)
  - regarding events between FS date and report date
  - that require adjustment or disclosure
- 🎯 Respond appropriately to facts becoming known after report date (Para 4b)
  - that may have caused amendment of report
  - had they been known at report date

## Scope
- Applies to: Auditor's responsibilities for subsequent events (Para 1)
- Does NOT apply to: Other information responsibilities (SA 720) (Para 1)
  - ⚠️ Exception: If other info reveals subsequent event (Para 1)
- Effective from: Audits beginning on/after April 1, 2009 (Para 3)
- Types of Events (Para 2):
  - Providing evidence of conditions existing at FS date
  - Providing evidence of conditions arising after FS date

## Definitions
- 📌 Date of the financial statements (Para 5a)
  - Date of end of latest period covered by FS
- 📌 Date of approval of the financial statements (Para 5b)
  - Date on which all statements and notes have been prepared
  - AND those with authority assert responsibility for them
  - 🔗 See Para A2 for process details
- 📌 Date of the auditor's report (Para 5c)
  - Date auditor dates report in accordance with SA 700
  - Cannot be earlier than date of approval
  - 🔗 See Para A3
- 📌 Date the financial statements are issued (Para 5d)
  - Date auditor's report and audited FS are made available to third parties
  - 🔗 See Para A4-A5
- 📌 Subsequent events (Para 5e)
  - Events occurring between FS date and auditor's report date
  - AND facts becoming known to auditor after report date

## Requirements
- Period 1: Events Up to Auditor's Report Date (Para 6-9)
  - 🎯 Shall perform audit procedures to identify events (Para 6)
    - Aim: Obtain sufficient appropriate evidence
    - Scope: Events requiring adjustment or disclosure
    - ⚠️ Limitation: No need to test matters already satisfactorily concluded (Para 6)
  - 🎯 Nature and Extent of Procedures (Para 7)
    - Cover period from FS date to report date (or near as practicable)
    - Take into account risk assessment
    - Shall include:
      - (a) Understanding management's procedures for identification
      - (b) Inquiring of management/TCWG regarding subsequent events
      - (c) Reading minutes of meetings held after FS date
      - (d) Reading latest interim FS (if any)
  - 🎯 Evaluation (Para 8)
    - If events identified: Determine if appropriately reflected in FS
  - 🎯 Written Representations (Para 9)
    - Request from management/TCWG
    - Confirm all subsequent events requiring adjustment/disclosure are adjusted/disclosed

- Period 2: Facts Known After Report Date But Before Issue (Para 10-13)
  - ⚠️ General Rule: No obligation to perform procedures after report date (Para 10)
  - 🎯 Exception: If fact becomes known that would have caused amendment (Para 10)
    - (a) Discuss with management/TCWG
    - (b) Determine if FS need amendment
    - (c) Inquire how management intends to address it
  - 🎯 If Management Amends FS (Para 11)
    - (a) Carry out necessary audit procedures on amendment
    - (b) Unless restricted amendment applies (Para 12):
      - (i) Extend all subsequent event procedures (Para 6-7) to new date
      - (ii) Provide new auditor's report (not dated earlier than approval)
  - 🎯 Restricted Amendment Scenario (Para 12)
    - Trigger: Law/regulations permit restricting amendment to effects of event
    - Auditor permitted to restrict procedures to that amendment
    - Reporting options:
      - (a) Dual Dating: Include additional date restricted to amendment
      - (b) New/Amended Report: Include EOM/OM paragraph regarding restriction
  - 🎯 If Management Refuses to Amend (Para 13)
    - Scenario: Auditor believes amendment needed but mgmt refuses
    - If Report NOT yet provided to entity:
      - Modify opinion (Qualified/Adverse) per SA 705 (Para 13a)
    - If Report ALREADY provided to entity:
      - Notify mgmt/TCWG not to issue FS to third parties (Para 13b)
      - If issued anyway: Take action to prevent reliance on report

- Period 3: Facts Known After FS Issued (Para 14-17)
  - ⚠️ General Rule: No obligation to perform procedures (Para 14)
  - 🎯 Exception: If fact becomes known that would have caused amendment (Para 14)
    - (a) Discuss with management/TCWG
    - (b) Determine if FS need amendment
    - (c) Inquire how management intends to address it
  - 🎯 If Management Amends FS (Para 15)
    - (a) Carry out procedures on amendment
    - (b) Review steps to inform recipients of old FS
    - (c) Unless restricted amendment applies:
      - (i) Extend procedures to new report date
      - (ii) Provide new report
    - (d) If restricted amendment applies: Follow Para 12
  - 🎯 Reporting on Amended FS (Para 16)
    - Include EOM/OM paragraph
    - Refer to note discussing reason for amendment
  - 🎯 If Management Refuses Steps (Para 17)
    - Notify mgmt/TCWG auditor will seek to prevent reliance
    - If steps still not taken: Take appropriate action (seek legal advice)

## Key Nuances and Exceptions
- ⚠️ Dual Dating (Para 12a)
  - Only permitted if law/regs allow restricted amendment
  - Limits auditor's liability for subsequent events to the specific note
- ⚠️ Management Refusal (Para 13, 17)
  - If mgmt refuses to amend or notify users
  - Auditor obligation shifts to "Preventing Reliance"
  - Often requires legal counsel
- ⚠️ No Obligation Zones (Para 10, 14)
  - Auditor has NO duty to hunt for events after report date
  - Duty triggers ONLY if fact "becomes known" passively

  - 📋 Sales or acquisitions of assets (occurred or planned)
  - 📋 Increases in capital or debt issuance
  - 📋 Agreements to merge or liquidate
  - 📋 Assets appropriated by government or destroyed (fire/flood)
  - 📋 Developments regarding contingencies
  - 📋 Unusual accounting adjustments
  - 📋 Events questioning appropriateness of accounting policies
  - 📋 Events relevant to measurement of estimates/provisions
  - 📋 Events relevant to recoverability of assets

## Logical Flow Chains
- Events After Report Date (Period 2)
  - Fact becomes known (Para 10)
    - leads to: Discussion and determination (Para 10a-b)
      - if Amendment required:
        - Mgmt amends?
          - YES: Procedures + New Report (Para 11)
          - NO: Modify Opinion or Prevent Reliance (Para 13)
- Events After Issue Date (Period 3)
  - Fact becomes known (Para 14)
    - leads to: Discussion and determination (Para 14a-b)
      - if Amendment required:
        - Mgmt amends?
          - YES: Procedures + Review notification steps + New Report (Para 15)
          - NO: Action to prevent reliance (Para 17)
