# SA 580: Written Representations

## Objective
- To obtain written representations from management (Para 5a)
  - That they fulfilled responsibility for financial statements
  - And for completeness of provided information
- To support other audit evidence (Para 5b)
  - If determined necessary or required by SAs
- To respond appropriately to written representations (Para 5c)
  - Or if management does not provide them

## Scope
- Applies to auditor responsibility for written representations (Para 1)
- Are necessary audit evidence but not sufficient alone (Para 2-3)
- Effective for periods beginning on/after April 1, 2009 (Para 4)

## Definitions
- 📌 Written representations (Para 6): A written statement by management provided to the auditor to confirm certain matters or to support other audit evidence. Written representations in this context do not include financial statements, the assertions therein, or supporting books and records.
- 📌 Management (Para 7): References to “management” should be read as “management and, where appropriate, those charged with governance.” Furthermore, in the case of a fair presentation framework, management is responsible for the preparation and fair presentation of the financial statements in accordance with the applicable financial reporting framework; or the preparation of financial statements that give a true and fair view in accordance with the applicable financial reporting framework.

## Requirements
- Management from Whom Requested
  - 🎯 Auditor shall request from responsible management (Para 8)
    - With appropriate responsibilities and knowledge of matters
- Representations about Responsibilities
  - 🎯 Shall request representation on preparation of FS (Para 9)
    - In accordance with applicable framework
    - 🔗 See SA 210 terms
  - 🎯 Shall request representation on info and transactions (Para 10)
    - Provided all relevant info and agreed access
    - All transactions recorded and reflected in FS
  - 🎯 Responsibilities shall be described exactly as in terms (Para 11)
- Other Written Representations
  - 🎯 Shall request representations required by other SAs (Para 12)
  - 🎯 Shall request others if necessary to support evidence (Para 12)
- Date and Period(s) Covered
  - 🎯 Date shall be near as practicable to auditor report (Para 13)
  - ⚠️ Shall not be after the date of auditor report (Para 13)
  - 🎯 Shall cover all FS and periods referred to (Para 13)
- Form of Written Representations
  - 🎯 Shall be in form of representation letter (Para 14)
  - ⚠️ Unless law requires written public statements (Para 14)
    - If such statements provide required representations
- Doubt as to Reliability
  - 🎯 Shall determine effect of management concerns (Para 15)
    - Regarding competence, integrity, or ethical values
  - 🎯 Shall perform procedures to resolve inconsistencies (Para 16)
  - ⚠️ If unresolved, shall reconsider management assessment (Para 16)
  - 🎯 Shall take action if representations are not reliable (Para 17)
    - 🔗 Determine effect on opinion per SA 705
- Requested Representations Not Provided
  - 🎯 If not provided, auditor shall: (Para 18)
    - Discuss the matter with management
    - Re-evaluate integrity and reliability of representations
    - Take appropriate actions including SA 705 effects
  - 🎯 Shall disclaim opinion on financial statements if: (Para 19)
    - ⚠️ Sufficient doubt about management integrity exists
    - ⚠️ Or management does not provide Para 9/10 reps

## Application Guidance
- Explains Paras 2-3 (Evidence Nature)
  - Modifying or refusing alerts to significant issues (A1)
  - Written requests prompt rigorous management consideration (A1)
- Explains Para 8 (Whom to request)
  - May be CEO and CFO or equivalent (A2)
  - Management expected to have sufficient knowledge (A3)
  - May include making inquiries of specialized individuals (A4)
  - May accept "to the best of its knowledge" qualifiers (A5)
  - Auditor may request confirmation of internal inquiries (A6)
- Explains Paras 9-10 (Responsibilities)
  - Other evidence alone is insufficient without confirmation (A7)
  - Auditor may ask management to reconfirm understanding (A8)
- Explains Para 12 (Other Reps)
  - May request reps on policies or specific assertions (A10)
  - May request communication of internal control deficiencies (A11)
  - Needed for judgments, intentions, and completeness evaluation (A12-A13)
- Explains Paras 9-10, 12 (Thresholds)
  - May consider communicating threshold for misstatements (A14)
  - 🔗 See SA 450
- Explains Para 13 (Date and Period)
  - Opinion cannot be expressed before representation date (A15)
  - Requires updating if obtained during audit course (A16)
  - May agree on form updating prior period reps (A17)
  - Requires coverage even if current management was absent (A18)
- Explains Para 14 (Form)
  - Public statements might satisfy requirement factors (A19)
  - Formal compliance statements alone are insufficient (A20)
- Explains Paras 9-10, 12 (TCWG Comm)
  - 🔗 See SA 260: Communicate requested written representations (A22)
- Explains Paras 15-16 (Reliability Doubt)
  - Inconsistencies may require revising risk assessment (A23)
  - Severe concerns may cause withdrawal if possible (A24)
  - 🔗 See SA 230: Document significant issues resolved (A25)
- Explains Para 19 (Not Provided / Disclaimers)
  - Unreliable reps cause pervasive lack of evidence (A26)
  - Modified reps do not automatically mean not provided (A27)
- Material Modifications to ISA 580
  - Deleted specific references to public sector entities applicability

## Key Nuances and Exceptions
- ⚠️ Para 13: Exception on dating — representations shall never be dated after the auditor's report date
- ⚠️ Para 14: Exception to representation letter — if law requires public statement that covers required matters
- ⚠️ Para 16: Exception on evidence — if inconsistency remains unresolved, auditor must reconsider overall management competence and integrity
- ⚠️ Para 19: Exception outcome — must explicitly disclaim opinion if Para 9 and 10 representations are refused or unreliable

## ICAI Listed Examples
- Para A4 — Examples of specialized individuals for inquiries
  - 📋 An actuary responsible for actuarially determined accounting measurements
  - 📋 Staff engineers who may have responsibility for environmental liability measurements
  - 📋 Internal counsel who may provide information essential to provisions for legal claims
- Para A8 — Examples of when to reconfirm responsibilities
  - 📋 Those who signed the terms no longer have relevant responsibilities
  - 📋 The terms of the audit engagement were prepared in a previous year
  - 📋 There is any indication that management misunderstands those responsibilities
  - 📋 Changes in circumstances make it appropriate to do so
- Para A10 — Examples of additional representations about financial statements
  - 📋 Whether the selection and application of accounting policies are appropriate
  - 📋 Plans or intentions that may affect carrying value or classification
  - 📋 Liabilities, both actual and contingent
  - 📋 Title to, or control over, assets, liens, encumbrances, collateral
  - 📋 Aspects of laws, regulations and contractual agreements affecting financial statements
- Para A12 — Examples of evaluating judgments and intentions
  - 📋 The entity's past history in carrying out its stated intentions
  - 📋 The entity's reasons for choosing a particular course of action
  - 📋 The entity's ability to pursue a specific course of action
  - 📋 The existence or lack of any other inconsistent information

## Logical Flow Chains
- Handling Doubtful Representations (Para 15-17)
  - Identified inconsistency or doubt (Para 15-16)
    - leads to: Perform audit procedures to resolve (Para 16)
      - if unresolved: Reconsider management assessment (Para 16)
        - leads to: Determine effect on evidence reliability (Para 16)
          - if concluded not reliable: Take appropriate actions (Para 17)
            - results in: Determining effect on opinion via SA 705 (Para 17)
- Handling Refusals (Para 18-19)
  - Management refuses requested representation (Para 18)
    - leads to: Discuss matter with management (Para 18a)
    - and: Re-evaluate integrity and evidence reliability (Para 18b)
      - if requested Para 9 or 10 reps still refused: Disclaim opinion (Para 19b)
      - if sufficient doubt about integrity arises: Disclaim opinion (Para 19a)
